LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight launched on May 22, 2026 in two editions: Standard and Deluxe. The Deluxe Edition includes the Legacy Collection — three themed packs of suits, Batmobiles, and Batcave props available from day one — plus a Season Pass covering the Mayhem Collection DLC releasing September 18, 2026. Here's exactly what each pack contains.

Standard vs Deluxe Edition

Standard Edition

The base game with all 5 chapters, 7 playable characters, and 101 base suits. Does not include any Legacy Collection content or access to the Mayhem Collection DLC.

  • Full story campaign (5 chapters, 29+ missions)
  • 7 playable characters
  • 101 base suits
  • 23 Red Bricks, all collectibles
Deluxe Edition

Everything in the Standard Edition plus the full Legacy Collection (3 themed packs) at launch, and a Season Pass for the Mayhem Collection DLC releasing September 18, 2026.

  • All Standard Edition content
  • Legacy Collection: 3 packs (21 suits, 3 Batmobiles, 15 Batcave props)
  • Mayhem Collection DLC (September 18, 2026) — included at no extra cost
  • Joker & Harley Quinn as playable characters when Mayhem drops

💡 Note: If you already own the Standard Edition, a Deluxe Edition Upgrade is available on all platforms. You can also purchase the Legacy Collection and Mayhem Collection individually as separate DLC purchases.

Legacy Collection — Available at Launch

The Legacy Collection is divided into three themed packs. Each pack follows the same format: 7 character suits (one for each playable character), 1 new Batmobile skin, and 5 Batcave customization props.

Batcave Props Included

The Arkham Trilogy Pack adds: Arkham Asylum Diorama, Arkham City Blueprints, Joker TV, Lady Gotham Statue, and Riddler Trophy. The Batman Beyond Pack adds: G.L.M., Eggbaby Crib, Bat Armor, Bat-Hound Doghouse, and Photo Op Board (Terrific Trio). The Party Music Pack adds its own set of five music-themed prop decorations for the Batcave hub.

Mayhem Collection — September 18, 2026

🃏 Mayhem Collection DLC — September 18, 2026

The Mayhem Collection is the first major post-launch DLC, adding two entirely new playable characters, a new story mission, and a new game mode. Deluxe Edition owners receive it at no extra cost.

  • Joker — playable character with chaos-themed gadgets
  • Harley Quinn — playable character with acrobatic combat style
  • New Story Mission: Break out of Arkham Asylum as Joker and Harley Quinn
  • Mayhem Mode: Cause chaos on Gotham's streets — an open-ended villain sandbox
  • Sinister Pack: 7 villain-inspired suits (one per original character), 1 Batmobile, 5 Batcave props

💡 Note: The Mayhem Collection releases on September 18, 2026 on all platforms simultaneously, including Nintendo Switch 2 (which also launches that day). Standalone pricing has not been announced. Standard Edition owners can upgrade to Deluxe for $24.99 on PS5, Xbox, and PC — this gives you Legacy Collection now and Mayhem Collection on September 18. Deluxe Edition holders receive the Mayhem Collection automatically at no extra cost.

Is the Deluxe Edition Worth It?

✅ Verdict: Worth it if you plan to replay the game

The 21 launch suits spread across three iconic Batman eras (Arkham, Beyond, and Party) are purely cosmetic — they don't affect gameplay. If you're a fan of the Arkham or Batman Beyond art styles, the skins look excellent in-game. The real value is the Mayhem Collection: two new playable characters plus a new story mission and game mode make a meaningful content addition, especially if you're planning to replay for 100% completion. For casual players who just want the story, the Standard Edition is perfectly complete.

The total DLC suit count with the full Deluxe Edition reaches 129 suits (101 base + 21 Legacy Collection + 7 Sinister Pack suits — one per original playable character). This is confirmed by our Suits Guide and multiple post-launch sources.

🎁 Free Bonus Suits for All Editions (Account-Linked): Regardless of which edition you own, you can unlock three free exclusive Batsuits by connecting accounts from the main menu: the Golden Age Batsuit (free WB Games account required), and the Dark Knights of Steel and Black Lantern Batsuits (active HBO Max subscription required). These are cosmetic-only unlocks available to all players on day one. Full instructions: Free Suits Guide.
🎮 Pre-Order Bonus — The Dark Knight Returns Batsuit: The Dark Knight Returns Batsuit (inspired by Frank Miller's 1986 comic series) was available as a pre-order bonus on all platforms — PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC, and Nintendo Switch 2. The pre-order window for PS5/Xbox/PC closed at the May 22, 2026 launch; players who pre-ordered on those platforms already have the suit. For Nintendo Switch 2, pre-orders remain active through the September 18, 2026 launch at $69.99 (Standard) / $89.99 (Deluxe) — the only currently open route to obtain this suit for new buyers. (Source: ComicBook.com; WB Games, June 3, 2026)
🧱 Switch 2 Physical Deluxe — Exclusive LEGO Minifigure: The physical Nintendo Switch 2 Deluxe Edition includes an exclusive Retro Video Game Batman Minifigure — a physical LEGO collectible inspired by the 1989 NES game Batman: The Video Game (Sunsoft). Available while supplies last with physical retail copies only; no digital equivalent exists. (Source: Stonewars)
