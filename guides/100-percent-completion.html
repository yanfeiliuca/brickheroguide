100% Completion Overview

What we know about reaching full completion in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight.

Achieving 100% completion in a LEGO game is always a major undertaking, and LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight is no exception. Based on preview coverage, the game features "abundant collectibles," suggesting completionists will have plenty to hunt for. A detailed 100% roadmap will be assembled after the full game launches on May 22, 2026.

What 100% Completion Will Likely Involve

Based on the LEGO game series formula and confirmed features, reaching 100% will probably require:

  • Completing all story missions
  • Finding all collectibles (Minikits, Red Bricks, and other hidden items)
  • Unlocking all 7 playable characters
  • Unlocking all vehicles in the Batcave
  • Completing all open-world activities and side content
  • Purchasing all upgrades

General Strategy

As with all LEGO games, the most efficient approach is to complete the story first, then revisit missions in Free Play mode with your full character roster to collect everything you missed. Story mode restricts you to specific characters, while Free Play lets you switch between any unlocked character to access all areas.
