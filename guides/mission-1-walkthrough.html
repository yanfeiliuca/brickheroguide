Mission 1: Shadows Over Gotham serves as the game's introduction, teaching you the core mechanics of combat, traversal, and puzzle-solving. You play as Batman solo for most of the mission, with Robin joining at the end. Despite being a tutorial level, there are plenty of collectibles hidden throughout — though some require Free Play characters to reach.

Mission Overview

Gotham City is under siege by Scarecrow's fear gas. Batman spots suspicious activity in the warehouse district and sets out to investigate from the rooftops. This mission introduces basic combat, Batarang targeting, Grapnel Gun traversal, and Detective Vision.

Story Context: This mission takes place on the night Batman first encounters Scarecrow's new fear toxin formula. The events here set the entire game's story in motion.

Available Characters

  • Batman — Available from the start. Use Batarang, Grapnel Gun, Glide, and Detective Vision.
  • Robin — Joins during the final cutscene after this mission (playable from Mission 2 onward).
Note: In Story Mode, you only have Batman for this entire mission. Minikits #3 and #5 require Robin (Tech Hack) and can only be collected in Free Play.

Part 1: Gotham City Rooftops

The mission begins on a rooftop overlooking the city. This section teaches you movement and basic combat.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Move forward along the rooftop. Smash the air conditioning units for studs. There are 3 units total — don't miss the one behind the water tower.
  2. Grapple up to the higher ledge using the grapple point (glowing orange hook icon). This is your first Grapnel Gun tutorial.
  3. Defeat the 4 thugs on the next rooftop. Use basic attacks and Batarang throws. The game will prompt you to try both.
  4. Use Detective Vision (press the special ability button) to reveal footprints leading to a hidden door. Follow the glowing trail.
  5. Build the LEGO lever from the bouncing bricks near the door. Pull the lever to open the rooftop access.
  6. Glide across the gap to the next building. Hold the jump button mid-air to activate the cape glide. Aim for the landing pad marked with studs.
🎲 Minikit #1: On the first rooftop, use Detective Vision to spot a cracked wall behind the water tower. Hit it with a Batarang 3 times to reveal a hidden alcove containing the minikit.

Part 2: The Warehouse District

After gliding down from the rooftops, Batman arrives in the warehouse district. This area is larger and introduces environmental puzzles.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Land in the alley and smash all crates for studs. The blue dumpster on the right contains a Gold Stud (100).
  2. Defeat the 6 thugs in the courtyard. Try to build up a combo by not getting hit — a 10+ combo earns bonus Skill Bricks.
  3. Solve the generator puzzle: Use Detective Vision to find the 3 fuse boxes. Hit each one with a Batarang in the correct order (left, right, center) to power up the warehouse door.
  4. Enter the warehouse. Smash everything inside — there's a massive amount of studs hidden in the barrels and shelving units.
  5. Navigate the fear gas room: Scarecrow's gas is filling the lower floor. Grapple to the upper catwalks and cross using the balance beams. Don't fall — the gas damages you.
  6. Build the ventilation fan from LEGO pieces on the far platform. Activating it clears the gas and reveals the exit door.
🎲 Minikit #2: In the warehouse, before clearing the fear gas, look up. There's a grapple point on the ceiling beam. Grapple up to find the minikit sitting on top of a high shelf. You need to do this BEFORE activating the fan — the camera angle changes after.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play Only): In the alley before the warehouse, there's a tech panel on the locked side door. Use Robin's Technology Hack to open it and find the minikit inside a small room.

Part 3: Confronting the Thugs

Beyond the warehouse, you enter a loading dock area where a larger group of enemies awaits.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Fight the 8 thugs in the loading dock. 2 of them are armored — use the Batarang to stun them first, then attack while they're dazed.
  2. Destroy the 3 supply crates marked with Scarecrow's logo. Each one drops a key piece.
  3. Build the Batcycle ramp from the 3 key pieces. Batman automatically uses it to smash through the reinforced gate.
  4. Chase the fleeing van through the short driving section. Steer left/right to avoid obstacles and press attack to throw Batarangs at the van. 5 hits stops it.
True Hero Tip: The loading dock has the highest stud density in this mission. Smash every single barrel, crate, and container before moving on. Combined with the supply crate studs, this should push you well past the 25,000 True Hero threshold.

Boss Fight: Scarecrow (Phase 1)

The mission's final encounter is your first boss fight against Scarecrow. This is a multi-phase tutorial boss designed to teach you the boss fight mechanics.

Phase 1: Fear Hallucination

  1. Scarecrow throws fear gas bombs that create glowing orange zones on the ground. Avoid the orange zones — they damage you and slow your movement.
  2. Wait for Scarecrow to stop and taunt. When he glows yellow, throw a Batarang at him. This stuns him temporarily.
  3. Run up and attack him 3-4 times while stunned. He'll retreat and the cycle repeats.

Phase 2: Giant Scarecrow Illusion

  1. Scarecrow transforms into a giant illusion (classic LEGO Batman moment). He slams the ground creating shockwaves.
  2. Jump over the shockwaves and grapple to the elevated platform when prompted.
  3. Use Detective Vision to spot the 3 weak points on the giant Scarecrow (glowing red nodes).
  4. Hit all 3 weak points with Batarangs. Each hit causes him to stagger.
  5. After all 3 hits, build the anti-toxin sprayer from the bouncing bricks. Activate it to end the illusion.

Phase 3: Final Takedown

  1. Scarecrow is disoriented. He throws random gas bombs wildly — they're easier to dodge but more frequent.
  2. Stun with Batarang, then attack — same pattern as Phase 1 but requires 5 hits instead of 3.
  3. After depleting his health, a cutscene plays. Robin arrives to help apprehend Scarecrow.
No-Damage Bonus: Defeating Scarecrow without taking any damage awards 10 bonus Skill Bricks for Batman. The attack patterns are very telegraphed — focus on dodging rather than rushing in.

All Minikit Locations

🎲 Minikit #1 (Story Mode): Part 1 — Rooftop. Use Detective Vision behind the water tower. Batarang the cracked wall 3 times.
🎲 Minikit #2 (Story Mode): Part 2 — Warehouse ceiling. Grapple to the ceiling beam BEFORE clearing the fear gas.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play): Part 2 — Alley side door. Requires Robin's Tech Hack.
🎲 Minikit #4 (Story Mode): Part 3 — Loading dock. Smash all 5 green barrels in the corners (not the Scarecrow crates). The 5th barrel is hidden behind the forklift.
🎲 Minikit #5 (Free Play): Boss arena. There's a vent on the left wall. Requires Robin's Vent Crawl to enter. The minikit is inside.

Red Brick Location

🔴 Red Brick — Stud Magnet: In Part 2, after entering the warehouse, look for a high platform on the right side that requires a double grapple (two grapple points in sequence). The Red Brick is on top. This unlocks the "Stud Magnet" extra — studs are automatically attracted to you from a larger radius. Costs 50,000 studs to activate at the Batcave.

Mission Challenges

Complete all 3 to earn bonus WayneTech Chips:

  1. "Night Vision" — Use Detective Vision 10 times during the mission. Easy to complete naturally. Just activate Detective Vision whenever you enter a new area.
  2. "Combo King" — Achieve a 25-hit combo. Best attempted in the loading dock (Part 3) where there are 8 thugs at once. Use Batarang stuns to keep your combo going without getting interrupted.
  3. "Untouchable" — Defeat Scarecrow without taking damage. Focus on dodging in the boss fight. All attack patterns are telegraphed with clear visual indicators.

Free Play Collectibles

Return in Free Play with these characters to get everything:

  • Robin — Required for Minikit #3 (Tech Hack) and Minikit #5 (Vent Crawl)
  • Any other character — No additional character-specific requirements in this mission

With Robin as your partner, you can collect everything in a single Free Play run through Mission 1.
