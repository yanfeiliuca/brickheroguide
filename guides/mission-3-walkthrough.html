Mission 3: Cold Justice introduces the second major villain of the game — Mr. Freeze. While Batman and Robin are still tracking Scarecrow's movements, a silent alarm at the Gotham Museum pulls them into a completely different crisis. This mission features ice-themed environmental puzzles, a new suit for Batman (the Thermal Suit), and one of the game's most memorable boss encounters. The difficulty ramps up noticeably here, so make sure you're comfortable with character switching before jumping in.

Mission Overview

A silent alarm triggers at the Gotham Museum of Natural History. Batman and Robin arrive to find the building encased in frost — Mr. Freeze is after a rare diamond housed in the museum's gem vault. The mission takes you through increasingly frozen halls as you chase Freeze deeper into the building. This level introduces ice physics (slippery floors), frozen enemies, and Batman's Thermal Suit.

Story Context: This mission is the first hint that Gotham's villains are working toward something bigger. Freeze mentions a "partnership" during the boss fight cutscene — pay attention to the dialogue for story clues that connect to Mission 5.

Available Characters

  • Batman — Batarang, Grapnel Gun, Glide, Detective Vision, Thermal Suit (new!)
  • Robin — Technology Hack, Vent Crawl, Acrobatic Poles, Magnetic Suit
New: Thermal Suit! Found in Part 2, Batman's Thermal Suit lets him melt ice walls, walk through frozen floors without slipping, and resist Freeze's cold blasts. You'll need it for almost every puzzle from this point forward.

Part 1: Gotham Museum Entrance

The mission begins outside the museum. The front doors are frozen shut and the courtyard is crawling with Freeze's henchmen in their distinctive blue parkas.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Defeat the 6 Freeze Henchmen in the courtyard. These enemies are tougher than Scarecrow's thugs — they have ice shields that block frontal attacks. Use Batarangs to break the shields first, then attack from behind.
  2. Smash the frozen fountain in the center of the courtyard. It drops a massive amount of studs and reveals bouncing LEGO bricks.
  3. Build a portable heater from the bouncing bricks. Push it along the guide rails toward the frozen museum doors.
  4. Robin hacks the heater's control panel to activate it. The heat melts the ice on the doors after a short cutscene.
  5. Enter the museum lobby. Smash the reception desk, benches, and display cases for studs. The security guard booth on the left has a Blue Stud (1,000).
🎲 Minikit #1: In the courtyard, there are 5 frozen lampposts around the perimeter. Smash all 5 with Batarangs — each one shatters into studs and a LEGO piece. Destroying all 5 spawns the minikit near the fountain.

Part 2: The Dinosaur Hall

The museum's famous Dinosaur Hall has been partially frozen by Freeze. Giant LEGO dinosaur skeletons tower over icy floors, and the room serves as a puzzle hub where Batman acquires the Thermal Suit.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Cross the icy floor carefully. Ice physics are introduced here — characters slide and have reduced control. Tap the movement stick gently rather than holding it.
  2. Grapple to the T-Rex skeleton's head (Batman). There's a platform on top with a Bat-Suit changing station frozen inside a block of ice.
  3. Robin crawls through the vent on the left wall to reach a back room. Inside, hack the climate control terminal to activate the overhead heating lamps.
  4. The heating lamps melt the ice block around the suit station. Switch to Batman and equip the Thermal Suit.
  5. Use the Thermal Suit to melt the ice wall blocking the exit on the far side. Hold the special ability button to activate the thermal beam — aim at ice surfaces to melt them.
  6. Clear the frozen enemies. Three henchmen are frozen solid near the exit. Melt them with the Thermal Suit for bonus studs (they won't fight back).
  7. Open the exit door by melting both ice locks simultaneously — you need to aim the thermal beam at each one. The second lock requires Robin to stand on a pressure plate to rotate it into position.
🎲 Minikit #2: After getting the Thermal Suit, melt the ice covering the Triceratops skeleton's base. A minikit is frozen inside the pedestal — the thermal beam reveals it.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play): The Pterodactyl skeleton hanging from the ceiling has a minikit on its back. Requires Nightwing's Wall Jump to reach the upper balcony, then glide across to land on the skeleton.
Stud Tip: The dinosaur skeletons are fully destructible. Smash them all for a huge stud payout — the T-Rex alone is worth over 5,000 studs. You can easily hit the True Hero target in this room if you're thorough.

Part 3: The Frozen Gallery

Beyond the Dinosaur Hall, the museum's art gallery has been transformed into an ice maze. Frozen walls create a labyrinth that shifts as you melt passages.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Enter the gallery and use Detective Vision immediately. The correct path through the ice maze is marked by faint heat signatures on the walls.
  2. Melt the first ice wall (Thermal Suit) to reveal a corridor. Two Freeze Henchmen ambush you — defeat them quickly.
  3. At the T-junction, go right first. Melt the wall to find a small room with a lever. Pull the lever to lower a chandelier in the main gallery, creating a grapple point.
  4. Return to the junction and go left. Robin uses the acrobatic poles to cross a frozen pit. On the other side, he hacks a terminal to extend a bridge for Batman.
  5. Cross the bridge and grapple up to the chandelier. From here, glide to the upper gallery balcony.
  6. On the balcony, melt the large ice sculpture blocking the staircase. This triggers a short cutscene — Freeze notices you and seals the Gem Vault entrance with a reinforced ice barrier.
  7. Build the thermal charge launcher from LEGO pieces scattered on the balcony (3 pieces total). Place it facing the vault door and activate it to blast through Freeze's barrier.
Tricky Puzzle: The thermal charge launcher requires all 3 LEGO pieces. One is hidden behind a painting on the right wall — use Detective Vision to spot it. Many players miss this piece and get stuck.

Part 4: The Gem Vault

The final area before the boss. The Gem Vault is a high-security room filled with laser grids, pressure plates, and display cases containing valuable gems.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Disable the laser grid. Robin hacks the security terminal on the left to turn off the first set of lasers. Batman uses Detective Vision to spot a second hidden terminal behind the far display case — destroy the case, then Robin hacks it.
  2. Cross the pressure plate corridor. Step on the wrong plates and ice blasts fire from the ceiling. The safe path is: left, center, right, right, center. Look for the slightly discolored tiles to identify safe plates.
  3. Defeat Freeze's elite guard — a single armored henchman with a freeze ray. Dodge his blasts (they temporarily freeze your character for 3 seconds) and attack from the sides. Batarang stuns work but take 2 hits instead of the usual 1.
  4. Smash the central display case to trigger the boss fight cutscene. Freeze arrives and grabs the Gotham Diamond.
🎲 Minikit #4: In the vault, destroy all 6 gem display cases (not just the central one). The minikit appears on the empty central pedestal after all cases are smashed.

Boss Fight: Mr. Freeze

Mr. Freeze is one of the toughest early-game bosses. His freeze ray and ice armor make direct attacks useless until you expose his weak point. This is a 3-phase fight that requires both Batman and Robin.

Phase 1: Breaking the Ice Armor

  1. Freeze patrols the room firing his freeze ray in sweeping arcs. Stay behind cover — the gem display pedestals block his shots.
  2. Freeze periodically freezes the floor in a wide area. Jump to avoid getting stuck. If frozen, mash the button prompt to break free faster.
  3. Switch to Batman's Thermal Suit and aim the thermal beam at Freeze's back. Hold it for about 3 seconds to melt a section of his ice armor. You need to melt 3 sections total.
  4. After each armor section melts, Freeze becomes enraged and does a ground pound that sends ice shockwaves in all directions. Jump over them.

Phase 2: The Freeze Dome

  1. Freeze creates an ice dome around himself and starts charging a massive freeze blast. You have about 20 seconds to stop him.
  2. Robin hacks the overhead sprinkler terminal to spray water on the dome, creating cracks.
  3. Batman hits the cracked dome with the Thermal Suit beam to shatter it completely.
  4. Freeze is stunned — attack him with melee combos. You can land about 8 hits before he recovers.

Phase 3: Desperation

  1. Freeze overcharges his freeze ray, firing rapid bursts instead of sweeps. The room starts freezing from the edges inward, shrinking your safe area.
  2. Melt the 2 ice generators on opposite sides of the room with Batman's Thermal Suit. This stops the room from freezing further.
  3. Robin builds a reflector device from the LEGO pieces that fall when the generators are destroyed. Place it in front of Freeze.
  4. Lure Freeze into shooting the reflector — his own freeze ray bounces back and freezes him solid.
  5. Cutscene: Batman smashes Freeze's helmet, confiscates the Gotham Diamond, and hands Freeze over to GCPD. Freeze mutters about "the real plan" before being taken away.
No-Damage Bonus: Completing the Freeze fight without taking damage awards 20 Skill Bricks. Phase 3 is the hardest — the rapid fire freeze blasts are fast. Stay mobile and keep the pedestals between you and Freeze at all times.

All Minikit Locations

🎲 Minikit #1 (Story Mode): Part 1 — Courtyard. Smash all 5 frozen lampposts to spawn the minikit near the fountain.
🎲 Minikit #2 (Story Mode): Part 2 — Dinosaur Hall. Melt the ice on the Triceratops pedestal with the Thermal Suit.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play): Part 2 — On the Pterodactyl skeleton. Requires Nightwing's Wall Jump to reach the upper balcony.
🎲 Minikit #4 (Story Mode): Part 4 — Gem Vault. Destroy all 6 gem display cases.
🎲 Minikit #5 (Free Play): Part 3 — Frozen Gallery. Behind the reinforced ice barrier on the balcony's left side. Requires Batwoman's Explosive Gel to break through, then melt the ice inside with Thermal Suit.

Red Brick Location

🔴 Red Brick — Minikit Detector: In Part 3 (Frozen Gallery), at the T-junction, melt the floor ice directly at the intersection. A hidden trapdoor is revealed. Drop down to find a small cave-like room beneath the gallery with the Red Brick. This unlocks the "Minikit Detector" extra — a visual indicator points you toward any minikits in your current area. Costs 200,000 studs to activate at the Batcave.
Worth the price! The Minikit Detector makes 100% completion much easier. If you've already activated the x2 Stud Multiplier from Mission 2, you'll earn the 200,000 studs back quickly. Combine both extras for maximum efficiency.

Mission Challenges

Complete all 3 for bonus WayneTech Chips:

  1. "Meltdown" — Melt 20 ice objects with the Thermal Suit. Very easy to complete naturally. The Dinosaur Hall and Frozen Gallery are packed with meltable objects. Use the thermal beam on everything that looks frozen.
  2. "Ice Cold Combo" — Achieve a 30-hit combo. Best attempted in Part 1's courtyard or the boss fight's Phase 1. Chain Batarang stuns into melee attacks without pausing.
  3. "Flawless Heist Prevention" — Complete the Gem Vault without triggering any ice traps. Memorize the pressure plate pattern (left, center, right, right, center) and don't step on wrong tiles. This also means avoiding Freeze's elite guard's blasts.

Free Play Collectibles

Return in Free Play with these characters:

  • Nightwing — Required for Minikit #3 (Wall Jump to Pterodactyl skeleton)
  • Batwoman — Required for Minikit #5 (Explosive Gel on reinforced ice barrier)

Good news — you only need one Free Play run. Bring Nightwing and Batwoman together to collect both minikits in a single pass through the mission.
