The Batcave Mural in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight is a large visual reward system — complete challenges and watch the mural fill in piece by piece. There are 43 challenges in total, ranging from trivial one-offs you complete naturally while playing to skill tests requiring specific upgrades and deliberate effort. Each challenge rewards between 5,000 and 20,000 Studs when cashed in at the green terminal in the Batcave. You do not need to complete all 43 — completing just 20 is enough to unlock the associated achievement.

How the Mural System Works

The challenge board is located in the Batcave — move west above the Batmobile garage and interact with the green computer terminal. Any challenge you have already completed (the game tracks progress automatically) appears here, and pressing X (PlayStation) or A (Xbox) cashes it in for Studs.

You can complete challenges as early as the Prologue, but the payout terminal does not open until Chapter 2. Challenges completed before Chapter 2 are still tracked and can be cashed in when the terminal unlocks. When a challenge completes, a yellow star flashes on screen with the challenge name.

The Batcave mural fills progressively as you complete and cash in challenges. You do not need all 43 for 100% game completion — but you do need all 43 to fully fill the mural. The Batcave Challenge achievement only requires 20.

Tips for Efficient Completion

  • Cash in challenges at the terminal regularly — the terminal shows completed-but-uncashed challenges, but your progress is tracked automatically regardless
  • Do the Easy challenges passively first — by the time you finish the main story, you will have completed at least 15–20 without trying
  • Do not spend chips on Focus ultimates until you need Utility Belt — Utility Belt requires all 14 Focus ultimates (168 chips total). Plan your WayneTech spending accordingly if you want 100%
  • Tough Guy is the longest grind — if you plan to do it, switch to Dark Knight Mode in Chapter 1 and leave it on throughout the story
  • Combo Crusader is easiest at crime events — Gotham crime events spawn 10–15 enemies at once, giving you plenty of targets to chain without the combo meter dropping
Efficiency tip: You only need 20 challenges for the achievement. Focus on the 15 Easy challenges in the list above, then add 5 from the Medium list that match your playstyle — you will hit 20 before the end of Chapter 3 without any grinding.
