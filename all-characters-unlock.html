LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features 7 fully playable characters, each with unique abilities and an upgradeable skill tree. Unlike previous LEGO games that offered massive rosters of simple characters, Legacy focuses on a smaller cast with much deeper gameplay mechanics. Here's every character, what they can do, and exactly how to unlock them.

Character Overview

All 7 characters fall into different gameplay roles. Some excel at combat, others at puzzle-solving or traversal. For 100% completion, you'll eventually need every character's abilities, so make sure to unlock them all as you progress through the story.

Tip: You start the game with only Batman. Other characters unlock progressively as you complete story missions. Focus on the main story first to build up your full roster.

1. Batman (Bruce Wayne)

🦇 Batman

Role:
All-Rounder / Combat
How to Unlock:
Available from start
Signature Gadget:
Batarang
Traversal:
Grapnel Gun, Glide Cape

Unique Abilities

  • Batarang Throw — Hit distant targets and switches. Can be upgraded to hit multiple targets at once.
  • Grapnel Gun — Reach high ledges marked with grapple points. Essential for vertical exploration.
  • Glide — Hold jump mid-air to glide across gaps. Covers more distance than any other character's jump.
  • Detective Vision — Reveal hidden objects, breakable walls, and footprint trails. Activated by pressing the special ability button.
  • Suit Swap — Access special Bat-suits at designated changing stations throughout levels for unique abilities (Power Suit, Sensor Suit, etc.).
🔓 Unlock: Batman is your default starting character. No unlock required.

2. Robin (Tim Drake)

🦸 Robin

Role:
Tech Specialist / Puzzle Solver
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 1
Signature Gadget:
Staff & Zip-Line
Traversal:
Acrobatic Poles, Small Vents

Unique Abilities

  • Technology Hack — Interact with tech panels and computer terminals to open doors, disable traps, and control machinery.
  • Vent Crawl — Squeeze into small openings that other characters can't access. Many collectibles are hidden behind vents.
  • Acrobatic Poles — Swing on horizontal poles to cross large gaps. Only Robin and Nightwing can use these.
  • Magnetic Suit — Walk on special magnetic walls and ceilings at designated suit stations.
🔓 Unlock: Automatically unlocked after completing Mission 1: Shadows Over Gotham. Robin joins your team during the mission's final cutscene.

3. Batgirl (Barbara Gordon)

🦸 Batgirl

Role:
Combat / Investigation
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 3
Signature Gadget:
Dual Batarangs
Traversal:
Grapnel Gun, Double Jump

Unique Abilities

  • Dual Batarangs — Throw two batarangs simultaneously, hitting two targets at once. Great for multi-switch puzzles.
  • GCPD Database — Scan crime scenes to reveal hidden clues and unlock bonus collectible hints.
  • Double Jump — A second mid-air jump allows reaching platforms that require precise vertical movement.
  • Martial Arts Combo — Extended combat chains that deal more damage than standard attacks.
🔓 Unlock: Automatically unlocked after completing Mission 3: Gotham City Sirens. Batgirl arrives as reinforcement during the boss fight.

4. Nightwing (Dick Grayson)

🕶 Nightwing

Role:
Agility / Traversal
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 5
Signature Gadget:
Escrima Sticks
Traversal:
Acrobatic Poles, Wall Jump

Unique Abilities

  • Wall Jump — Bounce between parallel walls to reach extreme heights. Unique to Nightwing.
  • Escrima Sticks — Electrified melee weapons that can also power electrical panels and generators.
  • Acrobatic Poles — Like Robin, Nightwing can swing on horizontal poles.
  • Speed Build — Assemble LEGO builds faster than other characters, useful in timed puzzle sections.
🔓 Unlock: Automatically unlocked after completing Mission 5: Bludhaven Burning. Nightwing joins the team in the post-mission Batcave cutscene.

5. Alfred Pennyworth

🤵 Alfred

Role:
Support / Utility
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 7
Signature Gadget:
Serving Tray (Shield)
Traversal:
Standard

Unique Abilities

  • Butler's Access — Open locked "service" doors found throughout Wayne properties and Gotham buildings. Many hidden rooms require this.
  • Repair — Fix broken LEGO machinery and vehicles that other characters cannot interact with.
  • Tea Service — Heal nearby allies and buff their attack power temporarily. Essential in tough Free Play challenges.
  • Serving Tray Block — A unique defensive ability that deflects projectiles back at enemies.
🔓 Unlock: Complete Mission 7: Siege of Wayne Manor. Alfred proves himself in combat during the manor defense sequence.

6. Catwoman (Selina Kyle)

🐱 Catwoman

Role:
Stealth / Agility
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 9
Signature Gadget:
Whip
Traversal:
Wall Climb, Whip Swing

Unique Abilities

  • Whip Swing — Latch onto whip points to swing across large gaps. Some areas are only reachable with the whip.
  • Wall Climb — Climb special textured surfaces that no other character can scale.
  • Stealth Mode — Turn invisible to bypass security cameras and laser grids. Required for several collectibles.
  • Cat Burglar — Open safes and locked containers that other characters cannot interact with. Many contain bonus studs or collectibles.
🔓 Unlock: Complete Mission 9: Rooftop Rendezvous. Catwoman switches allegiance and becomes a playable ally.

7. Batwoman (Kate Kane)

🦸 Batwoman

Role:
Heavy Combat / Demolition
How to Unlock:
Complete Mission 11 (final story mission)
Signature Gadget:
Explosive Gel
Traversal:
Grapnel Gun, Glide

Unique Abilities

  • Explosive Gel — Place and detonate explosives on cracked walls and silver LEGO objects. The only way to break silver objects outside of suit stations.
  • Military Training — Superior combat damage and unique finishing moves. Best raw combat character in the game.
  • Tactical Vision — Similar to Batman's Detective Vision but also highlights enemy weaknesses and hidden explosive points.
  • Grapnel & Glide — Same traversal abilities as Batman, making her a versatile replacement in Free Play.
🔓 Unlock: Complete Mission 11: Legacy of the Dark Knight (the final story mission). Batwoman joins after the credits roll.

Tips for Choosing the Right Character

When replaying missions in Free Play mode, picking the right characters saves a lot of time. Here are some practical suggestions:

  • For general exploration: Batman or Batwoman (grapnel + glide covers the most ground)
  • For collectible hunting: Catwoman (stealth + wall climb + safe cracking reaches the most hidden areas)
  • For tech puzzles: Robin is the only option (tech hack + vent crawl)
  • For vertical areas: Nightwing (wall jump reaches places no one else can)
  • For silver objects: Batwoman is the only character with Explosive Gel
  • For locked rooms in Wayne buildings: Alfred with Butler's Access
Pro Tip: In Free Play, you can bring any two characters. The ideal pair for most missions is Catwoman + Robin — together they cover stealth, tech, vents, wall climb, and safe cracking. Switch to Batman + Batwoman for missions heavy on grapple points and silver objects.
