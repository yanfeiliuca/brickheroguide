LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight — Release Date, Platforms & Editions

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight is one of the most anticipated LEGO games of 2026, bringing together the Dark Knight's entire mythos in brick form. Here's everything you need to know about when it launches, which platforms you can play it on, and what edition is right for you.

Official Release Date

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight launches on May 22, 2026. The game releases worldwide simultaneously across all platforms, meaning no staggered regional delays. This is a global launch — players in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia all get access on the same day.

The game went gold in late April 2026, confirming no last-minute delays. Developer TT Games has a strong track record of hitting announced dates, and all indications point to a smooth launch.

Tip: Digital pre-loads typically go live 48 hours before launch. If you pre-order digitally, you can download the game ahead of time and start playing immediately at midnight local time on May 22.

Available Editions Compared

The game comes in two editions. Here's what you get with each:

The Character Pass includes four post-launch DLC packs featuring characters from across the wider DC universe. Each pack adds 2-3 playable characters with unique abilities. If you plan to aim for 100% completion and want the full roster, the Deluxe Edition's $15 premium is worth it.

Our Take: If you're a completionist or a Batman superfan, go Deluxe. The Character Pass alone would cost more if purchased separately post-launch. If you just want to play through the story, Standard is perfectly fine.

Pre-Order Bonuses

Regardless of which edition you choose, all pre-orders (digital or physical) include the Adam West Classic Batman Character Pack, which gives you:

  • 1966 TV Series Batman (playable character with unique Batusi taunt)
  • Classic Batmobile (1966 TV series model, usable in Gotham free roam)
  • Pow! Bam! Wham! visual effect studs

These bonuses are not locked to Deluxe Edition — any pre-order qualifies. Post-launch, this pack will be sold separately for $4.99.

Where to Buy

The game is available through all major retailers and digital storefronts:

  • Digital: PlayStation Store, Microsoft Store, Nintendo eShop, Steam, Epic Games Store
  • Physical: Amazon, GameStop, Best Buy, Target, Walmart, and other major retailers

Physical copies include a reversible cover featuring classic comic book art. The Switch physical cartridge contains the full game with no additional download required.

Cross-Platform Play & Cross-Save

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight supports local couch co-op on all platforms, which has always been a staple of LEGO games. Online co-op is also included across all platforms.

However, there is no cross-platform play — you can't play online co-op between a PS5 player and a PC player. Cross-save and cross-progression are also not supported. Your save file stays on the platform where you created it.

Tip: If you're deciding between platforms, go with the one where you'll play most consistently. Since there's no cross-save, switching platforms mid-playthrough means starting over.
