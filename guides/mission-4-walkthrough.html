LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight — Chapter 4 Walkthrough: Firefly, Batgirl Begins, Mr. Freeze

Chapter 4 of LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight is one of the most pivotal sections of the game, introducing two new playable characters and featuring a memorable boss encounter with Mr. Freeze. The chapter contains four missions: Firefly, Batgirl Begins, Out of Commission, and Mr. Freeze. Collectibles in this chapter include WayneTech Caches, Spaceships, Snow Globes, and Red Bricks.

✅ Confirmed for shipped game: Mission names, collectible types, and character unlocks on this page reflect the released game (May 22, 2026), verified against Push Square's mission list and walkthrough guides.

Character System Overview

Chapter 4 Overview

Chapter 4 is composed of four missions and marks the midpoint of the game. Two key character unlocks happen here — Batgirl joins your roster during "Batgirl Begins," and Nightwing is introduced at the conclusion of the Mr. Freeze boss fight. Collectibles across the chapter include WayneTech Caches, Spaceships (in Firefly), and Snow Globes (in Mr. Freeze).

Mission: Firefly

The first mission of Chapter 4 pits Batman against Firefly (Garfield Lynns), a jetpack-equipped pyromaniac. The mission emphasizes aerial traversal and ranged Batarang combat against a fast-moving flying opponent. Stay mobile and use Batarangs to stagger Firefly between his attack runs.

Firefly Collectibles

The Firefly mission contains WayneTech Caches, Spaceships, and a Red Brick. Spaceships are the mission's themed collectible — look for them mounted on walls and hidden in alcoves throughout the mission area. WayneTech Caches require Batman's Detective Vision gadget to locate.

Tip: In Free Play, bring a character with a ranged ability to hit Spaceships mounted in high or hard-to-reach positions. Nightwing's wall-jumping ability is particularly useful for reaching elevated collectible spots during replay.

Mission: Batgirl Begins

This story mission formally introduces Batgirl (Barbara Gordon) as a playable character. The mission leans into her tech specialization — expect hacking sequences, security bypass puzzles, and Batgirl's superior tech-access abilities coming into play for the first time. Completing the mission adds Batgirl to your roster permanently.

The mission functions as a character tutorial for Batgirl's kit. Take time to practice her hacking ability on every available terminal, as it unlocks collectibles in subsequent missions that no other character can reach on a first pass.

Mission: Out of Commission

The third mission of Chapter 4 bridges the Batgirl introduction with the Mr. Freeze confrontation. Specific collectible types and full walkthrough details for "Out of Commission" are less extensively documented in early guides — treat this mission as a story beat leading into the chapter's climax. Ensure you have fully explored and collected in the Firefly mission before proceeding, as returning during Chapter 4 is easiest before chapter completion locks certain areas.

Mission: Mr. Freeze — Walkthrough & All Collectibles

The Mr. Freeze mission is Chapter 4's climax and one of the game's most memorable boss encounters. The mission concludes with Nightwing's introduction — he appears after the boss fight and becomes immediately playable. The Mr. Freeze mission contains 5 Snow Globes and 1 Red Brick.

Snow Globe Locations (All 5)

All 5 Snow Globes are found after the Mr. Freeze boss fight, in the post-combat arena area. Finding all 5 awards the "Snow Globes Batcave Trophy."

  • Snow Globe #1 & #5: Attached to the left side of the machine and behind it. Both are accessible after Nightwing's introduction.
  • Snow Globe #2: On the ledge behind your starting position in the boss arena.
  • Snow Globe #3: Attached to the wall on your left after the boss fight.
  • Snow Globe #4: On the right side of the boss arena.
Tip: None of the Snow Globes require special character abilities — they can all be collected with Batman and Nightwing immediately after the boss fight ends. Do not leave the arena before grabbing all five.

Red Brick Location

The Red Brick in Mr. Freeze requires both Batgirl and Nightwing:

  • Use Batgirl's hacking ability on the wall behind you in the boss arena.
  • Switch to Nightwing and hit the three targets that appear. The Red Brick pops out when all three are hit.

This Red Brick is obtainable during your first playthrough since both characters are available at this point in the story. Do not skip it — Red Bricks in Legacy of the Dark Knight are cosmetic unlocks (colour skins for suits and vehicles), not gameplay modifiers or Stud multipliers. See our Collectibles Guide for the full Red Brick list.

Chapter 4 Collectibles Summary

Here's a quick reference for all collectible types across Chapter 4:

  • Firefly mission: WayneTech Caches, Spaceships (exact count not confirmed in early guides), Red Brick
  • Batgirl Begins: Story mission — collectible details not fully documented yet
  • Out of Commission: Collectible details not fully documented yet
  • Mr. Freeze: 5 Snow Globes, 1 Red Brick (all post-boss fight)
Free Play tip: Return to Chapter 4 missions in Free Play after unlocking all characters to collect any ability-gated items you missed. Catwoman's lockpicking and Nightwing's wall-jump both access spots that may not have been reachable during the story run.
