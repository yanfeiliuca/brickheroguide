Tips for New Players in LEGO Batman Legacy of the Dark Knight

Last updated: May 19, 2026 · 4 min read

New to LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight -- or new to LEGO games in general? This guide covers the most important things to know before you start, with special attention to what makes this game different from previous titles.

General LEGO Game Tips

If you have played a LEGO game before, much of the core loop will feel familiar. Destroy everything in sight to collect studs (the in-game currency). Studs are used to purchase characters, vehicles, and upgrades. The more you smash, the more you earn.

Do not stress about missing collectibles on your first playthrough. Story mode locks you into specific characters, and many hidden items are only accessible in Free Play mode after you have unlocked more of the roster. Play through the story first, then revisit missions to clean up.

What Makes This Game Different

This is not your typical LEGO game. Here are the key differences to be aware of:

  • Arkham-inspired combat: The free-flow counter system is deeper than classic LEGO button-mashing. Learn to time counters and use character-specific abilities in fights.
  • Smaller roster, deeper characters: With only 7 playable characters (compared to dozens in previous titles), each character has more distinct abilities that you will actually need to learn and use.
  • UE5 visuals: Built on Unreal Engine 5, the game looks significantly better than any previous LEGO title. Expect longer load times on HDD; an SSD is recommended.
  • Local co-op only: There is no online multiplayer. Drop-in/drop-out local co-op is available for a second player on the same screen.

Exploration Tips

  • Check every rooftop: Gotham is vertically dense. Grapple and glide to rooftops -- they frequently hide collectibles.
  • Use character abilities: Each of the 7 characters can access different areas. Switch characters to reach blocked-off sections.
  • The Batcave is your hub: Between missions, return to the Batcave to customize vehicles, switch Batsuits, and track your collectible progress.
  • Replay missions in Free Play: After unlocking more characters, replay earlier missions to collect everything you missed.

Combat Tips

  • Learn to counter: The Arkham-style combat system rewards well-timed counters. Watch for enemy attack indicators and respond quickly.
  • Switch characters for different situations: Some enemies are easier to handle with specific characters. Experiment with your roster.
  • You cannot permanently die: Like all LEGO games, dying only costs you studs. You respawn at checkpoints. This is a low-stakes game -- experiment freely.

Performance & Setup Tips

  • Use an SSD: The UE5 engine benefits significantly from fast storage. Load times on HDD will be noticeably longer.
  • Check your GPU: As a current-gen-only UE5 title, older graphics cards may struggle. A GPU with 6 GB+ VRAM is recommended for smooth 1080p performance.
  • Controller recommended: Like all LEGO games, a controller provides the best experience. The game supports Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo controllers on PC.
Key takeaway: Play the story first, enjoy the ride, and do not worry about 100% completion until you have unlocked all characters. The game is designed to be replayed -- rushing to find everything on your first run will only slow you down.
