Characters

Best Characters for Each Mission

Optimize your team for every challenge Gotham throws at you.

Choosing the right character for the job can mean the difference between struggling and soaring. While you can beat any story mission with the default characters, certain heroes excel in specific environments. Here's our breakdown.

Mission 1: The Gotham Docks

Top Pick: Nightwing. His agility and escrima sticks allow him to navigate the tight platforms of the docks with ease. His ability to hack basic security panels also opens up early shortcuts.

Mission 2: Chemical Plant Infiltration

Top Pick: Robin. His Hazard Suit is essential for navigating the toxic spills in the plant. He can also use his grapple to pull down heavy valves and clear paths for the rest of the team.

Mission 3: Arkham Asylum Escape

Top Pick: Batman. The Dark Knight's stealth abilities are vital for avoiding the asylum's advanced security systems. His Batarangs are also perfect for hitting distant switches to release locked doors.
