All 7 Playable Characters & Their Confirmed Abilities

Last updated: May 19, 2026 · Based on official announcements, developer interviews, and early reviews

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features 7 playable characters — a deliberate departure from previous LEGO games that offered hundreds of characters. Developer TT Games explained they wanted to focus on deeper gameplay systems, with each character having distinct abilities, rather than a large roster of similar characters. Here's every confirmed playable character and what we know about them.

Source: The 7-character roster was confirmed by TT Games at Gamescom 2025 and on the official game website. Specific abilities have been reported in preview coverage and early reviews.

Character Overview

The 7 playable characters are: Batman, Jim Gordon, Batgirl, Nightwing, Robin, Catwoman, and Talia al Ghul. Each possesses unique skills and gadgets that the player will need to use throughout the game's open-world Gotham City and story missions.

1. Batman (Bruce Wayne)

🦇 Batman

Voice Actor:
Shai Matheson
Role:
All-Rounder / Combat
Availability:
Available from start
Traversal:
Grappling Hook, Gliding

Confirmed Details

  • Grappling Hook & Gliding — Traversal across Gotham City's open world is done through grappling hooks and gliding. Batman can also use the Batmobile.
  • Batarangs & Gadgets — The combat system encourages using a combination of abilities and gadgets like batarangs alongside environmental actions, inspired by the Arkham series' freeflow combat (but more approachable).
  • Multiple Batsuits — Batman can wear different Batsuits inspired by various Batman media, including skins based on the likeness of actors who have played the character in films.
Available from the start of the game.

2. Jim Gordon

👮 Jim Gordon

Based on:
Jeffrey Wright's portrayal (The Batman, 2022)
Role:
Ranged / Utility
Signature Gear:
Foam Gun

Confirmed Details

  • Foam Gun — Gordon carries a gun that sprays foam, a non-lethal weapon consistent with the LEGO games' family-friendly approach.
  • GCPD Connection — As Gotham's police commissioner, Gordon likely has access to police-related interactions and areas within the city.
  • The character model is based on Jeffrey Wright's likeness from the 2022 film The Batman.
Unlock conditions have not been detailed in pre-release coverage. Likely unlocked early in the story.

3. Batgirl (Barbara Gordon)

🦸 Batgirl

Role:
Combat / Tech
Identity:
Barbara Gordon

Confirmed Details

  • Batgirl is one of the core Bat-Family members in the roster. Specific gameplay abilities have not been detailed in pre-release coverage, but she is expected to share some traversal and combat capabilities with Batman while having her own unique tools.
Unlock conditions TBD. More details will be added after the game's full release.

4. Nightwing (Dick Grayson)

🕶 Nightwing

Role:
Agility / Acrobatics
Identity:
Dick Grayson (former Robin)

Confirmed Details

  • Nightwing, the original Robin who became his own hero, is a playable character. His acrobatic background from his circus days and his independent crimefighting career in Bludhaven are expected to inform his gameplay style.
Unlock conditions TBD.

5. Robin (Tim Drake)

🦸 Robin

Role:
Tech Specialist
Identity:
Tim Drake

Confirmed Details

  • Tim Drake, the third Robin in DC Comics continuity, is the game's Robin. In the comics, Tim is known for his detective skills and technological prowess, which may translate into unique puzzle-solving abilities.
Unlock conditions TBD.

6. Catwoman (Selina Kyle)

🐱 Catwoman

Based on:
Michelle Pfeiffer's portrayal (Batman Returns, 1992)
Role:
Stealth / Agility
Signature Gear:
Whip

Confirmed Details

  • Whip — Catwoman uses a whip as a weapon. It may also serve as a traversal tool or for interacting with specific objects in the environment.
  • Her character skin is based on Michelle Pfeiffer's iconic portrayal from Tim Burton's Batman Returns (1992).
Unlock conditions TBD.

7. Talia al Ghul

💇 Talia al Ghul

Role:
Stealth / Combat
Affiliation:
League of Shadows (initially)

Confirmed Details

  • Talia al Ghul is the daughter of Ra's al Ghul. Given the game's premise retells Batman's origin from his time training with the League of Shadows, Talia likely plays a significant role in the story — potentially starting as an antagonist before becoming playable.
Likely unlocked later in the story. Exact trigger TBD.

Ally Characters (Non-Playable)

Not every member of the Bat-Family is playable. The following characters appear in the game but are not part of the 7-character playable roster:

  • Alfred Pennyworth — Bruce Wayne's loyal butler, provides guidance from the Batcave.
  • Lucius Fox — Wayne Enterprises executive, likely involved in tech and gadget development.

Villains

Batman's rogues gallery features prominently in the game. Confirmed villain characters include:

🧠 The Riddler
🌈 Poison Ivy
💀 Bane (voiced by Matt Berry)
🦈 The Penguin
😈 The Joker (Heath Ledger-inspired)
❄ Mr. Freeze
🔥 Firefly
🍯 Condiment King
👻 Ra's al Ghul
🔫 Two-Face
🤯 Egghead
🌄 Kite Man
🤑 Carmine Falcone
👿 Sofia Falcone

Additional characters include Thomas and Martha Wayne, Gillian B. Loeb, and the reality-warping imp Bat-Mite.

💡 Why only 7 characters? TT Games made a deliberate choice to feature a smaller roster compared to previous LEGO games. According to developer interviews, this allowed them to focus on deeper gameplay systems with each character having their own distinct abilities, rather than hundreds of characters with similar movesets.
