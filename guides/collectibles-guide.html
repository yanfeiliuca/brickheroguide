Collectibles Overview -- What to Expect at Launch

Everything we know so far about collectibles in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight.

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features a variety of collectibles scattered throughout its open-world Gotham City and story missions. However, detailed location guides cannot be compiled until after the full game launches on May 22, 2026, when players have had time to fully explore every area. Here is what we know so far from reviews and previews.

What Reviews Tell Us About Collectibles

Based on preview coverage, collectibles are abundant -- suggesting this game follows the LEGO tradition of packing the world with things to find. Like previous TT Games titles, you can expect to hunt for hidden items across missions and the open world.

Types of Collectibles (To Be Confirmed)

Based on the LEGO game series tradition and confirmed game features, players will likely hunt for the following:

  • Minikits: Hidden miniature models, traditionally 10 per level, that unlock when you find them all in a mission.
  • Red Bricks / Extras: Game-modifier collectibles that unlock cheats like stud multipliers and other bonuses.
  • Vehicles: Unlockable vehicles stored and customized in the Batcave.
  • Character Tokens: Used to unlock the game's 7 playable characters.
Coming after launch: Full collectible location guides -- including Minikit positions, Red Brick locations, and vehicle unlocks -- will be published after the May 22 launch. Bookmark this page and check back.

Best Approach to Collecting

As with all LEGO games, the recommended strategy is to play through the story first without worrying about missing collectibles. Most hidden items are only accessible in Free Play mode with character abilities unlocked later in the game. Finish the story, unlock your full roster, and then revisit levels to clean up.
