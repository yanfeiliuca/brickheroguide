About BrickHeroGuide

BrickHeroGuide is a dedicated fan site created to provide the most comprehensive and easy-to-use guides for LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight. Our mission is to help players of all skill levels find every secret, unlock every character, and achieve 100% completion in Gotham City.

We are a small team of passionate LEGO game enthusiasts who believe that every mission should be an adventure. Our guides are built from the ground up, with a focus on clarity, accuracy, and SEO-friendly content to ensure you find exactly what you're looking for.
