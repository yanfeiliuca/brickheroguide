All Characters & Villains in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features 7 playable heroes in the base game and 14 confirmed villains, with Joker and Harley Quinn joining as playable characters via the Mayhem Collection DLC. This page gives you an at-a-glance overview of every character — for deep dives, follow the links to the dedicated guides.

The 7 Playable Heroes

Each hero has a unique traversal ability and gadget set. Swapping characters freely from the Batcave is available after the prologue. For full ability breakdowns and unlock conditions, see the All Characters & Unlocks Guide.

🦇 Batman

Available from start

Grapnel Gun, cape glide, Batmobile summoning, Explosive Gel, Batarang. The all-rounder — no ability gating blocks Batman from most content.

💓 Batgirl

Unlocked: Chapter 4 — Batgirl Begins

Terminal hacking (required for Batgirl towers and many Riddler rooms), tech-enhanced gliding, Drone gadget. Essential for 100% — her tower hacks reveal all district collectibles.

🧰 Nightwing

Unlocked: end of Chapter 4 — Mr. Freeze

Wall-jumping between parallel surfaces, Electric Cable Launcher, faster ground movement. Excellent for rooftop traversal and the Mr. Freeze boss fight.

🦸 Robin

Unlocked: Chapter 2

Vent crawling through maintenance shafts, acrobatic pole swinging, insulated boots (negates electric hazards). Required for several collectible routes inaccessible to Batman.

🐈 Catwoman

Unlocked: Chapter 2 — Mines mission

Whip-swinging from anchor points, lockpicking (opens sealed question-mark boxes), silent stealth movement. Lockpick is required to reach several Red Bricks and Riddler caches.

👮 Jim Gordon

Unlocked: Chapter 1

Police vehicle access, evidence-gathering for Cluemaster Investigations (Gordon-exclusive open-world chains), GCPD area access. Required to complete all Cluemaster side-quests.

⚔️ Talia al Ghul

Unlocked: start of Chapter 5

League of Shadows parkour (Ninja Dash), Blowdarts, sword environmental interactions. The fastest melee fighter in the roster. Crosses over from villain to hero after Chapter 4.

Which character should I use? For most story content, Batman covers everything. For open-world 100%, you need Batgirl (towers), Catwoman (lockpick), and Gordon (Cluemaster chains) in rotation. See Best Characters for Each Mission for mission-by-mission picks.

Mayhem Collection DLC Characters (September 18, 2026)

The Mayhem Collection DLC adds Joker and Harley Quinn as the 8th and 9th fully playable characters, each with unique movesets, a new story mission (Arkham breakout), and access to Mayhem Mode. The DLC is included with the Deluxe Edition or available separately. See the Mayhem Collection DLC Guide for full details.

🤪 The Joker

DLC — September 18, 2026

Unique gadgets and chaotic combat abilities. Fully playable in the open world and the new Arkham breakout story mission. Visual design blends Jack Nicholson and Heath Ledger portrayals.

🥸 Harley Quinn

DLC — September 18, 2026

Acrobatic melee kit, oversized mallet, and her own unique traversal moves. Joins Joker in the Arkham breakout mission. Full ability breakdown available after DLC launch.

Character Selection Tips

  • Story mode: The game assigns characters per mission — you can always have Batman as active character, with story-required heroes as your partner.
  • Free Play mode: After completing a mission, replay it in Free Play to bring any unlocked character. This is the only way to reach ability-gated collectibles with the right character.
  • Open-world sweep order: Unlock Batgirl (Ch. 4) before sweeping any district — her tower hacks are the single biggest time-saver in the open world. Then prioritise Catwoman's lockpick and Gordon's evidence-gathering.
  • Suits and skins: Cosmetic Batsuits change Batman's appearance but not abilities. See the Suits & Abilities Guide for all confirmed skins.

Voice Cast Overview

Due to the 2024–2025 SAG-AFTRA video game strike, Legacy of the Dark Knight features a completely new voice cast. Notable highlights include Shai Matheson as Batman, Matt Berry as Bane (leaning into a Tom Hardy parody), Colin McFarlane (the real-life Commissioner Loeb from Nolan's Dark Knight trilogy) as Jim Gordon, and Ewan Bailey as The Joker. Archive audio of Kevin Conroy (as the Arkham Asylum Announcer) and Adam West (1960s archive footage) appear as tributes. See the Complete Voice Cast Guide for the full list of all confirmed actors.
