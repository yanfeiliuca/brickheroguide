Mission 2: Fear Factory is your first mission with both Batman and Robin working together. Set in the Ace Chemicals plant where Scarecrow is mass-producing fear toxin, this level introduces character-switching, Robin's unique abilities, and cooperative puzzles. Most importantly, this mission contains the x2 Stud Multiplier Red Brick — arguably the single most valuable collectible in the entire game.

🔴 Don't miss the x2 Stud Multiplier! This Red Brick is located in Part 2 (The Production Floor). Finding it early and activating it at the Batcave doubles ALL stud earnings for every future mission. See the Red Brick section for exact location.

Mission Overview

Following the events of Mission 1, Batman and Robin track Scarecrow's fear toxin supply to its source: the abandoned Ace Chemicals plant on Gotham's industrial waterfront. The factory has been converted into a mass production facility for the new fear formula. Your objective: shut down the operation and destroy the toxin supply.

Available Characters

  • Batman — Batarang, Grapnel Gun, Glide, Detective Vision, Suit Swap
  • Robin — Technology Hack, Vent Crawl, Acrobatic Poles, Magnetic Suit
Character Switching: Press the switch button to swap between Batman and Robin at any time. Many puzzles require both characters — Robin hacks the terminal while Batman pulls the lever, etc. Get comfortable switching frequently.

Part 1: Ace Chemicals Exterior

The mission begins at the factory gates. This section introduces cooperative puzzle-solving.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Approach the main gate. It's locked with an electronic lock. Switch to Robin and use Technology Hack on the panel to the right of the gate. A mini-game appears — match the circuit pattern to unlock.
  2. Enter the courtyard. Defeat the 6 thugs patrolling the area. Use Robin's staff for crowd control — his spinning attack hits multiple enemies.
  3. Find the 3 valve wheels. One is behind the guard booth (smash it), one is on top of the containers (Batman grapple up), and one is inside the vent to the left (Robin's Vent Crawl).
  4. Place all 3 valves on the pipe mechanism near the factory door. Turn them to redirect the chemical flow and open the blast door.
🎲 Minikit #1: In the courtyard, there are 5 green chemical barrels scattered around the area. Destroy all 5 (they glow slightly). The last one is inside the guard booth — smash the booth door first.

Part 2: The Production Floor

The factory's main production floor is a large, multi-level area with conveyor belts, chemical vats, and plenty of enemies. This is the mission's biggest section and contains the critical x2 Stud Multiplier.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Cross the conveyor belts. They move left-to-right. Time your jumps between gaps in the moving chemical containers. Robin's smaller size makes this slightly easier.
  2. Defeat the 2 armored guards at the control station. Use Batarangs to stun, then attack.
  3. Hack the control station with Robin to stop the conveyor belts and lower a bridge.
  4. Cross the bridge and smash the equipment on the far side. Build a Bat-Suit changing station from the pieces.
  5. Switch Batman to the Power Suit at the station. The Power Suit lets you pull heavy orange handles and throw explosive Bat-bombs.
  6. Pull the orange handle on the large pipe to redirect chemicals away from the exit. Then throw a Bat-bomb at the cracked wall to create a shortcut.
  7. Navigate the upper catwalks. Robin swings across the acrobatic poles while Batman grapples. Meet at the far platform.
  8. Team puzzle: Robin stands on the left pressure plate, Batman on the right. Both plates must be pressed simultaneously to open the exit.
🎲 Minikit #2: On the upper catwalks, after Robin crosses the acrobatic poles, look behind the last pole — there's a small platform with the minikit. You need to jump backward off the pole to reach it.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play): On the production floor, there's a silver LEGO container near the first conveyor belt. Requires Batwoman's Explosive Gel to destroy. The minikit is inside.

Part 3: The Ventilation System

The exit from the production floor leads into the factory's ventilation system — a network of ducts and fans that Robin navigates while Batman finds an alternate route.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Robin enters the vent at the base of the wall. Navigate through the duct system — follow the green arrows painted on the walls.
  2. Avoid the spinning fans — wait for them to slow down, then pass through quickly. Getting hit knocks you back but doesn't cause much damage.
  3. Robin emerges in a control room. Hack the terminal to disable the security lasers blocking Batman's path in the corridor below.
  4. Switch to Batman and walk through the now-clear corridor. Use Detective Vision to spot the hidden switch on the wall — pulling it opens Robin's exit door.
  5. Reunite and proceed through the double doors to Scarecrow's lab.
Stud Tip: The ventilation ducts contain a surprising amount of studs. Smash every grate and pipe fitting as Robin moves through. There's a Blue Stud (1,000) hidden behind the second fan.

Part 4: Scarecrow's Lab

The final area before the boss is Scarecrow's personal laboratory where the concentrated fear toxin is stored.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Defeat the 10 thugs in the lab. This is a bigger fight — focus on building combos for Skill Brick bonuses. Robin's staff and Batman's Batarang stuns are your best tools.
  2. Destroy the 4 toxin tanks around the room. Each requires a different approach:
    • Tank 1: Pull the orange handle (Batman's Power Suit)
    • Tank 2: Hack the release valve (Robin's Tech Hack)
    • Tank 3: Throw a Batarang at the weak point (visible in Detective Vision)
    • Tank 4: Build a device from LEGO bricks and activate it
  3. Destroying all 4 tanks triggers the boss fight.
🎲 Minikit #4: In Scarecrow's Lab, use Detective Vision to find a hidden safe behind the bookshelf on the right wall. There's no way to open it in Story Mode — it requires Catwoman's Cat Burglar ability in Free Play.

Boss Fight: Fear Toxin Machine

Instead of fighting Scarecrow directly, you face his automated Fear Toxin Machine — a massive LEGO contraption that fills the room with gas while deploying robotic drones.

Phase 1: Disable the Gas Vents

  1. The machine activates 3 gas vents around the room, creating toxic zones. Robin hacks each vent's control panel to shut them down one by one.
  2. While Robin hacks, Batman defends him from waves of drones. Use Batarangs to hit distant drones and melee for close ones.
  3. After all 3 vents are disabled, the machine's core is exposed.

Phase 2: Destroy the Core

  1. The exposed core has 3 glowing orange weak points. Batman throws Batarangs at each one.
  2. After each hit, the machine retaliates with a sweeping laser beam — jump over it.
  3. Hit all 3 weak points to trigger the destruction sequence.

Phase 3: Escape

  1. The machine explodes and the factory starts collapsing. Run toward the exit following the on-screen prompts.
  2. Robin hacks the final door while Batman holds off the last wave of enemies.
  3. Cutscene: Batman and Robin escape as the factory crumbles. Scarecrow's main production facility is destroyed, but he's still at large.
No-Damage Bonus: Completing the boss fight without taking damage awards 15 Skill Bricks split between Batman and Robin. The laser sweep in Phase 2 is the hardest to dodge — it always sweeps left to right, so position yourself on the right side.

All Minikit Locations

🎲 Minikit #1 (Story Mode): Part 1 — Courtyard. Destroy all 5 green chemical barrels. Last one is inside the guard booth.
🎲 Minikit #2 (Story Mode): Part 2 — Upper catwalks. Jump backward from the last acrobatic pole to a hidden platform.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Free Play): Part 2 — Silver container near first conveyor belt. Requires Batwoman's Explosive Gel.
🎲 Minikit #4 (Free Play): Part 4 — Hidden safe behind bookshelf in Scarecrow's Lab. Requires Catwoman's Cat Burglar.
🎲 Minikit #5 (Free Play): Part 1 — On the factory roof. Requires Nightwing's Wall Jump between two parallel walls near the gate to reach the rooftop.

Red Brick Location (x2 Stud Multiplier!)

🔴 Red Brick — x2 Stud Multiplier: In Part 2 (Production Floor), after Batman changes into the Power Suit, look to the LEFT instead of going right toward the exit. There's a heavy orange handle on a sealed cabinet. Pull it open with the Power Suit to reveal the Red Brick. This is the most important collectible in the entire game — it doubles all stud earnings permanently once activated at the Batcave for 500,000 studs.
Don't leave this mission without it! The x2 multiplier pays for itself within 2-3 missions. Every stud you earn from this point forward is doubled. If you missed it, replay Mission 2 immediately.

Mission Challenges

Complete all 3 for bonus WayneTech Chips:

  1. "Dynamic Duo" — Switch characters at least 20 times. Easy — just tap the switch button frequently throughout the mission.
  2. "Speed Chemist" — Destroy all 4 toxin tanks in under 60 seconds. This requires knowing the tank order in advance. Start with the Batarang tank (#3) while heading toward the Power Suit handle (#1), then Tech Hack (#2), then build (#4).
  3. "Efficient Escape" — Complete the escape sequence without taking damage. Stay in the center of the corridor during the collapse and jump over every debris pile.

Free Play Collectibles

Return in Free Play with these characters:

  • Batwoman — Required for Minikit #3 (Explosive Gel on silver container)
  • Catwoman — Required for Minikit #4 (Cat Burglar safe in lab)
  • Nightwing — Required for Minikit #5 (Wall Jump to factory roof)

You'll need at least two Free Play runs (since you can only bring 2 characters), or combine Catwoman + Nightwing for minikits #4 and #5, then a separate run with Batwoman for #3.
