Batcave Hub Guide — Your Customizable Home Base

Last updated: May 19, 2026 · 4 min read

The Batcave is your central hub and customizable home base in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight. It serves as the launching point for missions and a showcase for everything you unlock throughout your adventure. The Batcave evolves as you progress — new vehicles, Batsuits, trophies, and collectibles populate the space, transforming it into a personalized gallery of your accomplishments across Gotham City.

Explore Regularly: Return to the Batcave between missions. As you unlock new content, previously empty areas fill with vehicles, suits, and collectibles on display. The Batcave is designed to grow with your progress.

Vehicle Display

🚗 Vehicle Collection

As you unlock vehicles throughout the game — including the Batmobile and other iconic transports from Batman's arsenal — they appear on designated display platforms within the Batcave. Each vehicle is rendered in full LEGO detail, and you can view them up close between missions.

Vehicles are unlocked through story progression, challenge completion, and exploration. The Batcave's vehicle display area grows alongside your collection, with additional platforms appearing as you add more vehicles to your garage.

Batsuit & Skin Display

👔 Batsuit & Skin Collection

Batman's multiple Batsuits and the various character skins you unlock — including actor-based skins inspired by Michelle Pfeiffer's Catwoman, Danny DeVito's Penguin, Heath Ledger's Joker, and Jeffrey Wright's Jim Gordon — are visible in the Batcave's display areas. This lets you appreciate the detail TT Games has put into each design.

Character skins and Batsuits are unlocked through story progression, challenge completion, and stud purchases. The Batcave provides a dedicated space to view and equip your collection before heading out on missions.

Trophies & Collectibles

🏆 Trophy & Collectible Displays

The Batcave features dedicated display areas for the trophies and collectibles you earn throughout the game. As you gather Minikits, Red Bricks, Gold Bricks, and other collectibles from missions across Gotham, they fill out display cases, pedestals, and wall mounts within the cave.

These visual indicators serve as satisfying reminders of your completion progress. The more you explore and collect, the more impressive and personalized your Batcave becomes, rewarding thorough players with a home base that reflects their dedication.
