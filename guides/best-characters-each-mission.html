Characters

Character Playstyle Guide

An overview of the 7 playable characters and their confirmed abilities.

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features 7 playable characters, a smaller roster than previous LEGO Batman games. This focused cast means each character has more distinct abilities and a unique playstyle. Here is what we know about each character based on confirmed previews and reviews.

Batman -- The All-Rounder

Batman is the most versatile character, with access to multiple Batsuits that grant different abilities. He can grapple, glide, use Batarangs, and switch between suits to solve puzzles and handle combat situations. Batman is the character you will use for most of the story.

Robin -- The Agile Technician

Robin provides agility-focused gameplay with acrobatic combat moves. He can access areas other characters cannot reach and brings technical abilities for puzzle-solving.

Batgirl -- The Stealth Operative

Batgirl brings stealth-focused abilities to the roster, complementing the more direct combat styles of other heroes. Her skills are useful for missions that reward a quieter approach.

Catwoman -- Whip & Stealth

Catwoman's whip is her signature tool, usable for both combat and traversal. Her stealth capabilities make her valuable in sections where avoiding detection is advantageous.

Commissioner Gordon -- Foam Gun Specialist

Gordon brings a unique foam gun tool that can neutralize certain hazards and enemies. His evidence-gathering abilities help uncover hidden clues and secrets throughout Gotham.

Talia al Ghul -- The Melee Specialist

Talia brings League of Shadows sword combat techniques and parkour abilities to the roster. She is confirmed to be a playable character with a complex narrative role tied to the League of Shadows storyline.

Nightwing -- The Acrobatic Fighter

Nightwing combines fast acrobatic combat with escrima sticks. His agility makes him excellent for navigating complex environments and handling groups of enemies with crowd-control techniques.

All 6 Chapters and Their Key Missions

The game is structured across a Prologue and 6 Chapters, each built around a primary villain encounter. Here's the full mission list alongside character recommendations confirmed by post-launch community guides (source: Push Square):

Prologue

Wayne Manor Grounds, Falcone Confrontation, The League of Shadows, Infiltration. You play exclusively as Batman. Focus on mastering the grapple hook and basic combat combo system before any other characters are introduced.

Chapter 1 — Red Hood Gang

Missions: New Beginnings, Tricorner Investigation, Riddle Me This, Riddler, Carmine Falcone, The Subwayne, Spelunking, A Family Friend, WayneTech R&D, Glider Calibration, The Red Hood Gang.

Best characters: Batman throughout. Gordon becomes available partway through — use his foam gun to neutralize Falcone's armored enforcers, which Batman's batarang struggles against. The Riddler puzzle chain (Riddle Me This → Riddler missions) is worth completing in sequence to earn WayneTech tokens that fund your early upgrades.

Chapter 2 — The Joker

Missions: Mimes, The Penguin, Flugelheim, Joker.

Best characters: Catwoman unlocks during this chapter — her grapple and agility make the Flugelheim Museum's multi-level puzzle sections much faster. Batman's Detective Vision is critical in the Joker mission for tracking Joker-gas vents before they incapacitate the team.

Chapter 3 — Rogues Gallery

Missions: Two-Face, Kite Man, Poison Ivy.

Best characters: Gordon's projectile upgrades (if purchased) shine against Two-Face's coin-flip shield mechanic. For Poison Ivy, Robin's ability to interact with plant-based environmental puzzles makes certain sections significantly shorter. Kite Man is a platforming-heavy mission where Catwoman's wall-run is useful.

Chapter 4 — Freeze and Batgirl

Missions: Firefly, Batgirl Begins, Out of Commission, Mr. Freeze.

Best characters: Batgirl becomes playable in "Batgirl Begins" and is essential for the Chapter 4 tower puzzles. Her ability to activate towers that reveal collectibles across entire districts makes her the single most valuable character for completionists. For Mr. Freeze, Batman's Thermal Batarang suit is the recommended loadout.

Chapter 5 — Arkham

Missions: Tumbler Chase, Arkham Asylum.

Best characters: Nightwing's acrobatic speed advantages appear in Tumbler Chase's on-foot sections. Arkham Asylum is the most combat-intensive chapter — Batman's fully upgraded grapple slam (unlocked mid-game) makes the shield-carrying asylum guards manageable.

Chapter 6 — League of Shadows

Missions: The League of Shadows Returns, The Dark Knight Returns.

Best characters: Talia al Ghul is the recommended character for League of Shadows combat encounters, as her parkour and sword combat are designed to match the League's fighting style. Batman in the full Dark Knight suit is required for the final confrontation.

General rule: Play your first run in Story Mode without worrying about character optimization — most collectibles require Free Play anyway, where you can switch to any unlocked character. Save targeted character selection for your post-game 100% run.
