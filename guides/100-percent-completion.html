100% Completion Checklist & Roadmap

Your ultimate guide to mastering LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight.

Achieving 100% completion in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight is a true test of a fan's dedication. It requires finding every secret, unlocking every character, and mastering every mission. Here is your definitive checklist.

The 100% Requirements

  • All 15 Story Missions: Complete the story mode.
  • True Hero Status: Earn "True Hero" in every story mission.
  • All 150 Minikits: Find all 10 Minikits in every mission.
  • All 15 Red Bricks: Find the hidden Red Brick in each mission.
  • All 7 Main Characters: Unlock all playable heroes.
  • All 20 Vehicles: Unlock and purchase every vehicle.
  • All Instruction Pages: Find all hidden blueprint pages.
  • Full WayneTech Upgrades: Purchase every skill and gadget upgrade.

Roadmap

Focus on the story first. Once you have the full roster of characters, return to each mission in Free Play to collect the Minikits and Red Bricks you missed. Use the stud multiplier Red Bricks as soon as you find them to make purchasing upgrades trivial.
