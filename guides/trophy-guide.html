Trophy Guide & Achievements: Roadmap to Platinum

Estimated difficulty: 3/10 · Approximate time: 25-30 hours.

Welcome to the LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight Trophy and Achievement Guide. This game follows the standard LEGO formula: a relatively easy list of tasks that mostly requires patience and exploration rather than high skill. If you're looking to earn every trophy and achieve that elusive Platinum, you've come to the right place.

Roadmap to 100%

Step 1: Complete the Story Missions

Simply play through the main campaign. Don't worry about missing collectibles yet, as most are unreachable on your first run. You will unlock all story-related trophies during this phase.

Step 2: Unlock All Characters

After the story, ensure you have all 7 main characters unlocked. You'll need their unique abilities to access every corner of Gotham in the next step.

Step 3: Free Play Cleanup

Revisit every mission in Free Play mode to collect all 10 Minikits and the hidden Red Brick in each level. This is the bulk of the work.

Step 4: Hub Exploration & Batcave Hub

Complete all activities in the Batcave hub, unlock all vehicles, and purchase all Red Brick extras.

Key Trophies

  • Legacy of the Bat: Earn all other trophies (Platinum).
  • The Hero Gotham Needs: Complete the story campaign.
  • Minikit Master: Collect every Minikit in the game.
  • Ultimate Extra: Collect and purchase all 20 Red Bricks.
  • Billionaire Philanthropist: Collect 1,000,000,000 studs.
