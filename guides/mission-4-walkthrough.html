Mission 4: Riddle Me This is the game's most puzzle-heavy level. The Riddler has built an elaborate underground lair beneath Gotham's sewer system, and he's kidnapped a city official to prove he's smarter than Batman. This mission is lighter on combat but packed with brain-teasers, pattern puzzles, and environmental challenges. If you've been breezing through fights, this mission will test a completely different skill set.

Heads up: This mission has the highest True Hero target so far (70,000 studs). Smash absolutely everything and don't rush through the puzzle rooms — many destructible objects are hidden behind puzzle solutions.

Mission Overview

Following Freeze's capture in Mission 3, Batman analyzes the clues from Freeze's cryptic comments about "the real plan." A riddle arrives at Wayne Manor via an encrypted signal — it leads Batman and Robin to the Gotham sewers, where they discover the Riddler's hidden headquarters. The mission is structured around three distinct puzzle trials that Riddler has designed specifically to test the Dynamic Duo.

Story Context: This mission reveals that Riddler is the one coordinating Gotham's villains. He's been manipulating Scarecrow and Freeze as part of a larger scheme. The intel Batman gathers here drives the plot for Missions 5-7.

Available Characters

  • Batman — Batarang, Grapnel Gun, Glide, Detective Vision, Thermal Suit, Power Suit
  • Robin — Technology Hack, Vent Crawl, Acrobatic Poles, Magnetic Suit
Suit Swapping is Key! This mission requires frequent suit changes for Batman. There are 3 Bat-Suit stations throughout the level. Remember: the Thermal Suit melts ice, and the Power Suit pulls heavy objects. You'll need both in the Three Trials section.

Part 1: The Gotham Sewers

The mission begins at a manhole entrance in downtown Gotham. The sewer system is dark, damp, and full of Riddler's traps. This section serves as the approach to Riddler's lair.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Drop into the sewers and immediately use Detective Vision. The correct tunnel is marked with a faint green question mark on the wall — follow these markers throughout this section.
  2. Cross the water channel. The current is too strong to swim. Build a bridge from LEGO debris piled on the left bank (bouncing bricks). Push the completed bridge across the channel.
  3. Navigate the pipe maze. Robin enters the large pipe on the right using Vent Crawl. Inside, he finds a valve that redirects water flow, draining a flooded section for Batman to walk through below.
  4. Defeat the 4 Riddler Thugs in green outfits. They're similar to regular thugs but occasionally throw question-mark grenades that stun you. Keep moving to avoid the grenades.
  5. Solve the sewer gate puzzle: There are 3 colored levers (green, yellow, red). Pull them in the order shown on the display panel above the gate: yellow, green, green, red. Wrong order resets and spawns 2 more thugs.
  6. Slide down the waterfall into Riddler's underground complex. This is a scripted sequence — just enjoy the ride.
🎲 Minikit #1: In the sewer tunnel, after crossing the bridge, look up. There are 3 green question mark panels on the ceiling. Hit all 3 with Batarangs in left-to-right order to spawn the minikit in the water channel.
Stud Tip: The sewer debris is packed with studs. Smash every pipe, every grate, and every pile of bricks. The water channel (after draining) has a Purple Stud (10,000) hidden under a breakable floor tile near the bridge.

Part 2: Riddler's Entrance Hall

Riddler's lair opens with a grand entrance hall covered in green neon question marks. This room contains the first major puzzle and introduces the "Riddler Lock" mechanic used throughout the mission.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Enter the hall and watch the cutscene. Riddler appears on multiple screens, taunting Batman and explaining his three trials. A hostage (the city official) is visible behind a glass cage.
  2. Examine the Riddler Lock on the main door. It's a rotating cylinder with symbols. You need to find 3 Riddler Tokens hidden in this room to unlock it.
  3. Token 1: Use Detective Vision to spot a hidden switch behind the left screen. Pull it to reveal a small LEGO puzzle — rearrange the 4 tiles to form a question mark shape. Completing it drops Token 1.
  4. Token 2: Robin hacks the terminal on the right side of the room. This activates a Simon Says-style pattern game on the floor tiles — green tiles light up in sequence, and you must step on them in the same order. The pattern is: center, top-left, bottom-right, top-right, center. Token 2 drops from the ceiling.
  5. Token 3: Batman grapples to the upper balcony. There's a series of 5 targets that appear briefly on the wall. Hit all 5 with Batarangs within the time limit (about 8 seconds). Token 3 appears on the balcony.
  6. Insert all 3 tokens into the Riddler Lock to open the main door to the Three Trials.
🎲 Minikit #2: In the Entrance Hall, after completing the Simon Says puzzle, step on the floor tiles in reverse order (center, top-right, bottom-right, top-left, center). A hidden compartment opens in the floor revealing the minikit.

Part 3: The Three Trials

The heart of the mission. Riddler has prepared three separate challenge rooms, each testing a different aspect of gameplay. You can complete them in any order, but the walkthrough follows left-to-right.

Trial 1: The Mind (Left Door)

A pure puzzle room with no enemies. The floor is a giant grid with colored tiles that shift when stepped on.

  1. Study the pattern on the back wall — it shows which color each tile should be. Stepping on a tile cycles its color: green → yellow → red → green.
  2. Work from the corners inward. Start with the 4 corner tiles. Each one only needs 1-2 steps to match the wall pattern.
  3. Use Robin for the center tiles — his smaller hitbox only triggers one tile at a time. Batman's larger hitbox sometimes triggers adjacent tiles.
  4. When all tiles match the pattern, the Riddler Trophy pedestal rises from the center. Grab the trophy to complete this trial.
Stuck on the grid? The solution from the corners: Top-left step 2x, Top-right step 1x, Bottom-left step 1x, Bottom-right step 2x. Then center row: left step 2x, center step 1x, right step 3x (full cycle back to green).

Trial 2: The Body (Center Door)

An obstacle course testing platforming and traversal skills. Timed sections add pressure.

  1. A 90-second timer starts when you enter. Don't panic — it's generous if you know the route.
  2. Grapple up the first wall (Batman) to the first platform. Pull the lever to extend Robin's acrobatic poles.
  3. Robin swings across the poles while Batman glides to the second platform below.
  4. Both characters hit their switches simultaneously (stand on pressure plates) to open the gate to the final stretch.
  5. Sprint through the final corridor — dodge the swinging pendulum blades (3 total, they follow a simple left-right pattern) and grab the Riddler Trophy at the end.
If time runs out, you restart the trial from the beginning, but you keep any studs collected. The timer is tight on the first attempt but becomes easy once you know the route.

Trial 3: The Spirit (Right Door)

A combat challenge with a puzzle twist. Riddler spawns waves of enemies while the room slowly fills with electric floor panels.

  1. Wave 1: 6 regular Riddler Thugs. Fight them normally while avoiding the 2 electrified floor panels (glowing yellow). Defeat all enemies before the next wave.
  2. Wave 2: 4 armored thugs + 2 grenadiers. More floor panels activate. Use the remaining safe zones and keep moving. Batarang the grenadiers first to stop their stun grenades.
  3. Wave 3: The room is almost entirely electrified. Only 3 safe tiles remain. Lure the final 4 enemies onto electric tiles — they take damage from the floor too. Stand on safe tiles and use Batarangs to fight at range.
  4. After Wave 3, the electricity shuts off. Grab the Riddler Trophy from the center of the room.
🎲 Minikit #3: During Trial 3 (The Spirit), before grabbing the Riddler Trophy, use Detective Vision. One of the wall panels has a cracked section. Throw a Batarang at it to reveal a hidden alcove with the minikit. You must grab it BEFORE picking up the trophy — the room locks after.

Part 4: The Trophy Room

Completing all three trials opens the path to Riddler's personal Trophy Room — a massive hall displaying stolen artifacts and his greatest achievements.

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

  1. Enter the Trophy Room. The hostage is visible in a suspended cage above. Riddler is on a throne-like platform at the far end.
  2. Navigate through the display cases. Each case has a different trap:
    • Case 1: Laser grid — Robin hacks the terminal to disable
    • Case 2: Spinning blade floor — use Batman's grapple to swing over
    • Case 3: Freeze trap (ice floor) — Batman's Thermal Suit melts a path through
  3. Reach the throne platform and pull the lever to lower the hostage cage. This triggers the boss fight.
🎲 Minikit #4 (Free Play): In the Trophy Room, one display case has a gold LEGO lock. Requires Catwoman's Cat Burglar ability to pick the lock. Inside is a minikit surrounded by gold studs.

Boss Fight: The Riddler

Riddler doesn't fight with brute force — his boss fight is a multi-stage puzzle battle. He controls the room remotely from his platform, activating traps and obstacles while you try to reach him. This is the most cerebral boss fight in the game.

Phase 1: The Quiz Show

  1. Riddler activates 4 screens around the room, each showing a different symbol. He calls out a riddle, and you have 10 seconds to hit the screen with the correct answer using a Batarang.
  2. There are 3 riddles in this phase. The answers are always the screen showing the item the riddle describes. The riddles change each playthrough, but the visual answers are always obvious (e.g., "I have wings but cannot fly" — hit the screen showing a building).
  3. Wrong answers electrify the floor briefly. Correct answers lower a barrier, letting you advance toward Riddler's platform.

Phase 2: The Gauntlet

  1. Riddler activates a series of moving platforms and rotating laser beams between you and his throne.
  2. Batman grapples to the first moving platform. Time the jump to the second platform when the laser beam swings away.
  3. Robin takes the lower path through a vent system. He emerges behind Riddler's platform and hacks the terminal to disable the lasers.
  4. Batman advances to Riddler's platform while Robin keeps the lasers disabled. Hit Riddler with a Batarang to stun him — he drops to the arena floor.

Phase 3: Direct Confrontation

  1. Riddler activates his Puzzle Staff — a cane that fires energy blasts and creates holographic decoys.
  2. 3 Riddler decoys appear. Use Detective Vision to identify the real Riddler (the real one has a faint orange outline). Hit the real one with a Batarang.
  3. Riddler is stunned — melee attack him 4-5 times. He escapes and summons decoys again.
  4. Repeat 3 times total. Each round adds more decoys (3, then 5, then 7) and the real Riddler moves faster.
  5. Final cutscene: Batman smashes the Puzzle Staff, frees the hostage, and finds files on Riddler's computer revealing the villain alliance's master plan — setting up the game's second half.
No-Damage Bonus: 25 Skill Bricks for completing the fight without taking damage. Phase 1 is the riskiest — take your time reading each riddle rather than guessing. The floor shock from wrong answers is the most common source of damage.

All Minikit Locations

🎲 Minikit #1 (Story Mode): Part 1 — Sewers. Hit all 3 green question mark ceiling panels with Batarangs in left-to-right order.
🎲 Minikit #2 (Story Mode): Part 2 — Entrance Hall. After completing the Simon Says puzzle, step on the tiles in reverse order.
🎲 Minikit #3 (Story Mode): Part 3 — Trial 3 (The Spirit). Use Detective Vision to find the cracked wall panel BEFORE grabbing the Riddler Trophy.
🎲 Minikit #4 (Free Play): Part 4 — Trophy Room. Gold LEGO lock on a display case. Requires Catwoman's Cat Burglar.
🎲 Minikit #5 (Free Play): Part 1 — Sewers. After the waterfall slide, there's a magnetic wall panel on the left. Requires Robin's Magnetic Suit to walk up the wall. Wait — Robin already has the Magnetic Suit! However, in Story Mode, the wall is blocked by silver LEGO debris. You need Batwoman's Explosive Gel in Free Play to clear it first, then use Robin's Magnetic Suit to reach the minikit above.

Red Brick Location

🔴 Red Brick — Fast Build: In Part 3, after completing Trial 1 (The Mind), don't leave the room immediately. Use Batman's Power Suit to pull the heavy orange handle on the back wall (there's a Bat-Suit station just outside the trial room). This opens a secret compartment containing the Red Brick. Unlocks "Fast Build" — LEGO construction animations complete 3x faster. Costs 100,000 studs to activate at the Batcave.
Quality of life upgrade! Fast Build doesn't sound exciting, but it saves significant time during 100% completion when you're building dozens of objects across every mission. Highly recommended for completionists.

Mission Challenges

Complete all 3 for bonus WayneTech Chips:

  1. "Riddle Master" — Answer all Quiz Show riddles correctly on the first try. Pay close attention to the riddle text. The visual symbols on the screens make the answers obvious if you read carefully. Don't rush.
  2. "Speed Trials" — Complete all Three Trials in under 5 minutes total. Trial 1 can be done in 30 seconds if you know the tile solution. Trial 2 has a 90-second timer. Trial 3 depends on how fast you defeat enemies. Practice the tile grid pattern from the tip above.
  3. "Decoy Detective" — Never hit a wrong Riddler decoy during Phase 3. Always use Detective Vision before throwing the Batarang. The real Riddler has an orange glow. Take your time — there's no timer in this phase.

Free Play Collectibles

Return in Free Play with these characters:

  • Catwoman — Required for Minikit #4 (Cat Burglar lock in Trophy Room)
  • Batwoman — Required for Minikit #5 (Explosive Gel on silver debris in sewers)

Both minikits can be collected in a single Free Play run. Bring Catwoman and Batwoman together for maximum efficiency.
