## 11. 90-Day Growth Recommendations

### Priority 1 — Backlink Acquisition (Weeks 1–4)
The single biggest lever. DR 0–3 means all good content gets suppressed regardless of quality.
- Submit to game wikis and resource round-ups (Fandom, Reddit r/legogaming, Discord servers)
- Reach out to gaming YouTubers doing LEGO Batman content for guide page mentions
- Publish a "definitive" free resource (e.g., interactive map, printable collectibles checklist) that earns natural links
- Guest post or comment authoritatively in gaming communities

### Priority 2 — Complete Mission Walkthrough Coverage (Weeks 1–8)
Only 4 of ~21 missions have dedicated walkthrough pages. PushSquare has chapter-by-chapter coverage. Each mission page targets a unique long-tail keyword cluster worth 200–2,000 monthly searches.
- Publish the remaining 17 mission walkthrough pages
- Add in-mission collectibles data (Red Bricks, Surf Boards, Bat-Symbols)

### Priority 3 — Schema Markup Implementation (Weeks 1–2)
Zero structured data currently. Adding FAQ schema, HowTo schema, and BreadcrumbList to guide pages could earn rich results and increase CTR by 20–40%.

### Priority 4 — DLC Content Preparation (Weeks 6–12)
The September 18 DLC launch is the biggest single traffic opportunity in the site's lifecycle.
- Pre-publish DLC-specific guide shells now (Joker abilities, Harley Quinn walkthrough, Mayhem Collection 100% completion)
- Target "Mayhem Collection" keyword clusters proactively
- Update existing DLC page with confirmed content details as they become available

### Priority 5 — Ad Revenue Optimization (Weeks 4–8)
- Replace Auto Ads with manually-placed In-Article ad units at the most visible positions
- Implement a sticky sidebar ad on guide pages (already have `.sidebar-box` class)
- Consider Ezoic once traffic exceeds 10,000 monthly sessions (RPM typically 2–3× AdSense)

### Priority 6 — Social & Community Signals (Ongoing)
- Create Reddit posts in r/legogaming, r/legovideoGame with guide links (provide genuine value)
- Twitter/X: post guide updates tied to game news
- Discord: engage in official LEGO Batman and TT Games communities
