Batman's suit-switching mechanic is the defining feature of LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight. With six distinct Batsuits, each with unique abilities, knowing when and how to use each suit is the key to mastering the game. This guide covers every suit, every ability, and the upgrade paths that make them even more powerful.

How Suit Switching Works

Batman can switch between suits at any time during gameplay using the D-pad. Each direction on the D-pad is mapped to a specific suit (configurable in settings). There's a brief 0.5-second animation when swapping, during which Batman is briefly vulnerable, so avoid switching mid-combat unless you have an opening.

Suits are unlocked progressively through the story. You start with the Standard and Armored suits, and unlock the remaining four as you complete missions and Batcave upgrades. By Mission 5, you should have all six suits available.

Quick Swap Tip: You can swap suits mid-glide and mid-grapple. This is essential for reaching certain collectibles — for example, grappling as Standard Batman then swapping to Thermal Suit mid-swing to break an ice wall at the grapple destination.

Standard Batsuit — The All-Rounder

Standard Batsuit

Unlocked
Available from game start
D-Pad Direction
Up (default)
Core Abilities
Batarang, Grapple Gun, Glide Cape, Basic Melee
Signature Move
Cape Stun — stuns nearby enemies with cape flourish

The Standard Batsuit is your default and most versatile option. It has no special strengths but no weaknesses either. The Batarang hits distant switches and stuns enemies. The Grapple Gun reaches high ledges and grapple points marked with a bat symbol. The Glide Cape lets you traverse gaps and descend safely from heights.

When in doubt, this is your fallback suit. If you're not sure what obstacle you're facing, start here and observe the environment for clues about which specialized suit you need.

Armored Batsuit — The Tank

Armored Batsuit

Unlocked
Available from game start
D-Pad Direction
Left (default)
Core Abilities
Heavy Melee, Riot Shield, Ground Slam, Explosive Resistance
Signature Move
Shield Charge — bulldozes through groups of enemies

The Armored Batsuit sacrifices speed for raw defensive power. Damage taken is reduced by 60%, making it the go-to suit for boss fights and heavy combat encounters. The Shield Charge can break through enemy block formations and destroy reinforced objects that resist standard attacks.

The trade-off: you can't glide or grapple in this suit, so it's purely for combat situations. Switch out of it for traversal sections.

Boss Fight MVP: Whenever you see a health bar at the top of the screen, switch to Armored Suit. The 60% damage reduction is the difference between breezing through and burning through your stud respawns.

Stealth Batsuit — The Ghost

Stealth Batsuit

Unlocked
Complete Mission 2: "Gotham Under Siege"
D-Pad Direction
Right (default)
Core Abilities
Active Camouflage, Silent Takedown, Laser Grid Pass-Through, Sound Muffling
Signature Move
Shadow Strike — instant takedown from camouflage state

The Stealth Batsuit introduces stealth mechanics to the LEGO Batman formula. Activating camouflage makes Batman nearly invisible for 8 seconds (upgradable to 15), allowing you to bypass security cameras, sneak past enemies, and pass through laser grids without triggering alarms.

Silent Takedowns eliminate enemies instantly without alerting nearby foes. This suit is essential for stealth sections in Missions 3, 5, and 7, as well as for accessing certain hidden areas marked with a stealth indicator (a crossed-out eye symbol).

Thermal Batsuit — The Elemental

Thermal Batsuit

Unlocked
Complete Mission 4: "Shadows of the Past"
D-Pad Direction
Down (default)
Core Abilities
Heat Beam, Ice Wall Melting, Fire Immunity, Thermal Vision
Signature Move
Heat Wave — AoE blast that melts all ice and freezable objects in range

The Thermal Batsuit is your key to elemental puzzles. Its Heat Beam melts ice walls, frozen switches, and ice-encased collectibles. Thermal Vision highlights heat signatures through walls, revealing hidden enemies, secret passages, and temperature-based puzzles.

Fire immunity means you can walk through flame hazards that would damage other suits, opening up alternate paths in several missions. In combat, the Heat Beam deals continuous damage to enemies weak to fire (like Clayface and Mr. Freeze).

Sonic Batsuit — The Disruptor

Sonic Batsuit

Unlocked
Complete Mission 6: "Crimson Reckoning"
D-Pad Direction
Right + Down combo press (default)
Core Abilities
Sonic Blast, Glass Shatter, Enemy Disorient, Echo Location
Signature Move
Sonic Boom — charged AoE blast that shatters all glass and stuns all enemies in a wide radius

The Sonic Batsuit uses sound waves to solve puzzles and control crowds. Its Sonic Blast shatters glass barriers, breaks sonically-sealed containers, and activates sound-sensitive switches. Echo Location pulses reveal invisible platforms and hidden structural weaknesses in the environment.

In combat, the Sonic Boom is the best crowd control tool in the game — it stuns every enemy in a 15-meter radius for 5 seconds, giving you time to pick off priority targets or revive a downed co-op partner.

Power Batsuit — The Heavy Lifter

Power Batsuit

Unlocked
Complete Mission 7: "The Dark Knight Falls"
D-Pad Direction
Left + Up combo press (default)
Core Abilities
Super Strength, Object Lift & Throw, Ground Pound, Reinforced Wall Break
Signature Move
Seismic Slam — ground pound that damages and knocks down all enemies in a wide area

The Power Batsuit is the ultimate problem solver for physical obstacles. Super Strength lets Batman lift and throw large objects (cars, shipping containers, giant LEGO blocks), smash through reinforced walls that resist even Red Hood's C4, and activate heavy pressure plates.

This suit is unlocked late in the story, which means you'll need to revisit earlier missions in Free Play mode to access power-locked areas. Many of the most valuable collectibles (Red Bricks, rare Minikits) are hidden behind Power Suit obstacles.

100% Completion Essential: If you're aiming for 100%, the Power Suit is mandatory. At least 12 Minikits and 4 Red Bricks across the game require Power Suit access. Budget time for a Free Play cleanup run after unlocking this suit.

Suit Upgrade System

Each suit has its own upgrade tree at the Batcave's Armor Workbench, purchasable with WayneTech Chips. Upgrades fall into three tiers:

TierCost (WayneTech Chips)Examples
Tier 1500 - 1,000Extended glide duration, faster grapple, basic damage boost
Tier 21,500 - 3,000Longer camouflage, heat beam damage+, sonic blast range+
Tier 35,000 - 10,000Unlimited glide, double shield health, seismic slam radius+

We recommend upgrading in this priority order for the smoothest experience:

  1. Standard Suit: Grapple speed and glide duration first (improves all traversal)
  2. Armored Suit: Shield health (makes boss fights significantly easier)
  3. Thermal Suit: Heat Beam range (lets you melt objects from safer distances)
  4. Power Suit: Seismic Slam radius (essential for late-game combat encounters)
  5. Stealth Suit: Camouflage duration (15 seconds instead of 8 is game-changing)
  6. Sonic Suit: Sonic Boom radius (quality of life, lowest priority)
