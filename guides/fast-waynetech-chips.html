Tips & Tricks

Fastest Way to Farm WayneTech Chips

Maximize your income and upgrade your team in record time.

In LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight, WayneTech Chips and Skill Bricks are the lifeblood of your progression. Without them, your characters will remain weak and your vehicle garage empty. Here are the best ways to farm currency fast.

1. The Multiplier Stack

The single most important tip: find the Red Brick stud multipliers (2x, 4x, 6x, 8x, 10x) as soon as possible. These stack multiplicatively. Once you have all five, you'll be earning currency thousands of times faster than a new player.

2. Mission 1: The Docks Loop

Early in the game, the Gotham Docks mission has several areas with high-density breakable objects. Replaying the first section of this mission can net you a significant amount of chips in just a few minutes.

3. Complete Optional Challenges

Each mission has several optional objectives, such as "True Hero" status or "Timed Build" challenges. These reward you with large bonuses of Skill Bricks and WayneTech Chips. Never skip them!
