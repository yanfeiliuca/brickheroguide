Collectibles Guide: Minikits, Red Bricks & Vehicles

Everything you need for 100% completion in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight.
📜 Table of Contents

  2. Red Bricks: Game Modifiers
  3. Vehicles: Unlock the Garage

In LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight, the journey doesn't end when the credits roll. For many fans, the real challenge begins with finding every single collectible hidden across Gotham City. This guide covers the three main types of collectibles: Minikits, Red Bricks, and Vehicles.

Minikits: The Ultimate Prize

Each mission contains 10 hidden Minikits. Finding all 10 in a mission unlocks a miniature LEGO model that is displayed in the Batcave's Trophy Room. Completing the entire collection is a major requirement for 100% completion.

Tip: Many Minikits are only accessible in Free Play mode using character abilities unlocked later in the story.

Red Bricks: Game Modifiers

Red Bricks are the most powerful collectibles in the game. Each one unlocks a special "Extra" in the pause menu, such as stud multipliers (2x, 4x, 6x, 8x, 10x), invincibility, or character detectors. There are 20 Red Bricks in total.

  • Stud Multipliers: Essential for buying expensive upgrades.
  • Minikit Detector: Reveals the location of nearby Minikits with an on-screen arrow.
  • Fast Build: Dramatically speeds up building LEGO objects.

Vehicles: Unlock the Garage

From the iconic Batmobile to the nimble Batwing, there are dozens of vehicles to unlock and drive. Some are unlocked by completing story missions, while others require finding hidden "Vehicle Instruction Pages" or purchasing them with WayneTech Chips in the Batcave hub.

Tips for Efficient Collecting

Don't try to find everything on your first playthrough. Story mode restricts you to specific characters, while Free Play allows you to switch between any character you've unlocked. Focus on finishing the story first to have the full toolkit available for your hunt.
