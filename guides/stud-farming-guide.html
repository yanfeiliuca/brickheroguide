You need over 1 million Studs to purchase all 101 base suits from the Bat-Mite Store. Without Stud Multiplier Red Bricks, this takes many hours of grinding. With all five Multiplier Red Bricks active simultaneously, the same content yields a ×3,840 multiplier — turning a 100-Stud drop into 384,000. Here's exactly how to do it.

Deluxe Edition owners: The Legacy Collection adds 21 cosmetic suits (Arkham Trilogy, Batman Beyond, and Party Music packs) at no additional Stud cost — they unlock automatically. The Mayhem Collection Sinister Pack (September 18, 2026) similarly unlocks 7 more suits without Stud purchases, bringing the total to 129 suits. Stud farming is only needed for the 101 base game suits sold in the Bat-Mite Store. See our Legacy Collection DLC Breakdown for details on what's included.

Why Farm Studs

Studs are used to purchase suits in the Bat-Mite Store, unlock Bat-Cave upgrades, and buy certain Red Bricks from the store side. The most expensive suits cost 250,000–500,000 Studs at base prices. With 101 suits to collect, the total bill exceeds 1 million without multipliers.

💡 First: Before farming, find and activate all 5 Stud Multiplier Red Bricks. Farming without multipliers active is massively inefficient. The multipliers are the most impactful investment you can make.

Stud Multipliers — Stack Them All

There are 5 Stud Multiplier Red Bricks in the game. They stack multiplicatively, not additively:

Red BrickMultiplierCombined Effect
Stud Multiplier ×2×2×2
Stud Multiplier ×4×4×8
Stud Multiplier ×6×6×48
Stud Multiplier ×8×8×384
Stud Multiplier ×10×10×3,840

With all five active, every Stud source in the game is multiplied by 3,840. A 100-Stud breakable object becomes 384,000 Studs. Activating these Red Bricks in the Bat-Cave is the single most impactful action for Stud farming.

Method 1: Stud Cache Loop

1
Stud Cache Fast Travel Loop
Easiest / Fastest Setup

The Stud Cache in the open-world Gotham replenishes each time you visit the Bat-Cave and return. With all multipliers active, each Cache yields approximately 30,000 Studs per collection.

  1. Ensure all 5 Stud Multiplier Red Bricks are active in the Bat-Cave.
  2. Collect the Stud Cache in Gotham (glowing Stud cluster in the open world).
  3. Fast travel to the Bat-Cave using the map or the Batmobile.
  4. Fast travel back to Gotham — the Cache respawns.
  5. Repeat indefinitely.

Method 2: High-Density Mission Replay

2
Mission Replay — Wayne Tower Vault
High Stud Density

Certain missions have extremely dense environments packed with breakable LEGO objects, enemy spawns, and collectible Studs. Replaying these in Free Play with all multipliers active generates millions quickly.

  1. Activate all 5 Stud Multiplier Red Bricks.
  2. Select a high-density mission in Free Play from the Bat-Cave mission board.
  3. Smash every breakable object — furniture, crates, vehicles, and environment props all generate Studs.
  4. Defeat all enemy waves (enemy drops are multiplied too).
  5. Exit mission and replay from the start once cleared.

Method 3: Vehicle Missile Farming

3
Batmobile Missile Farming in Gotham
Open World

In Gotham's open world, spawning a Batmobile and using vehicle missiles to destroy environmental objects (wrecked cars, structures, barrels) generates large Stud clusters that are auto-collected when you drive through them.

  1. Activate all Stud Multiplier Red Bricks.
  2. Summon a Batmobile in the open world.
  3. Drive through dense areas of Gotham and use vehicle missiles on parked cars and large objects.
  4. Drive over the Stud drops — the Batmobile auto-collects nearby Studs.
  5. Find a dense street loop and repeat.

Method 4: Jim Gordon Rebound Launcher (Splitter Upgrade)

4
Gordon's Splitter Rebound Launcher
Mid-Game Unlock

After upgrading Jim Gordon's Rebound Launcher with the Splitter mod at the Workbench, his projectile splits on contact and ricochets multiple times — hitting far more LEGO objects per shot than any other gadget. In open-world areas with dense breakables, this becomes a high-efficiency Stud-generating tool.

  1. Unlock the Splitter upgrade for Gordon's Rebound Launcher at the Bat-Cave Workbench.
  2. Take Gordon into any open area of Gotham with dense LEGO breakables.
  3. Fire the Rebound Launcher repeatedly — the splitting projectile smashes many objects per shot.
  4. Collect Studs and repeat.

Priority Order for New Players

If you're starting fresh and want Studs fast, follow this order:

  1. Find the ×2 Multiplier Red Brick first — even a single ×2 doubles every Stud you earn immediately.
  2. Stack multipliers as fast as possible — prioritize Red Brick hunting in the open world over mission completion to get the compounding effect early.
  3. Use the Stud Cache Loop once all 5 multipliers are active — it's the lowest-effort method for consistent Stud income.
  4. Replay dense missions in Free Play for burst farming when you need a large Stud sum quickly.

💡 Remember: With all multipliers active at ×3,840, buying all 101 suits becomes a matter of 20–30 minutes of casual open-world play rather than hours of grinding. The multiplier setup is the whole game.

💰 Multiplier Math

  • ×2 brick alone = ×2
  • ×2 + ×4 = ×8
  • + ×6 = ×48
  • + ×8 = ×384
  • All 5 active = ×3,840
