Reaching 100% completion in LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight requires clearing every mission, collecting every Red Brick and Batcave collectible, completing all open-world side activities across Gotham's districts, unlocking all suits, and fully upgrading the Batcave. The good news: nothing is permanently missable. The Batcomputer in your Batcave lets you replay any mission at any time. Here is a phase-by-phase guide to getting there efficiently.

Recommended playstyle: Play through the full story on any difficulty without stressing about collectibles. Then use the post-game phase approach below. You will be better equipped with traversal abilities and a clearer map of what is left.

Phase 1: Story Playthrough

Play all 6 chapters and 29+ main missions in order. Focus on enjoying the story and learning the combat system. Collect Studs naturally but do not hunt for collectibles — you will need story-unlocked abilities to reach many of them on replay.

At the end of this phase you will have: all 7 playable characters, the Batmobile, grapple and glide traversal abilities, access to the full Gotham open world, and all Batcave rooms unlocked. Expect 12–18 hours of story content.

Phase 2: Open-World Gotham Completion

Gotham City is divided into distinct districts. Work through each district and clear all side activities before moving on. The in-game map marks outstanding activities per district. Side activities include:

  • Escaped Zoo Animals — find and capture animals scattered across each district
  • AR Trials — timed combat and traversal challenges accessed via AR terminals
  • Riddler Puzzle Rooms — logic and environmental puzzles that reward Gold Bricks and occasionally suits
  • Villain Encounters — open-world boss fights that unlock Gold Bricks on completion
  • WayneTech Upgrades — find and spend WayneTech Chips from the Batcave caches

Completing all open-world activities is the main time investment for 100% and covers the majority of Gold Bricks not tied to mission content. According to community guides at Push Square, this phase takes 8–15 hours depending on thoroughness.

Phase 3: Mission Replay & Collectible Mop-Up

Use the Batcomputer to replay any missions where you missed collectibles. Key targets in mission replay:

  • Any Red Bricks you did not collect during the story (one per chapter area)
  • Gold Brick challenge objectives — each mission has optional challenge goals that reward a Gold Brick
  • Stud score targets — reaching the Stud threshold for a mission's True Hero rank (if applicable)

By this phase, your Stud Multiplier Red Bricks should be active, making Stud targets much easier to hit on replay.

Phase 4: Batcave & Store Completion

Finish the remaining items that require accumulated Studs and collectibles:

  • Purchase all remaining suits from the Bat-Mite Store (total cost: ~1,005,000 Studs)
  • Buy the 9 store Red Bricks not found in the world
  • Find all 10 Batcave Minikits and all 10 WayneTech Caches within the Batcave
  • Complete all Batcave upgrades using Gold Bricks and WayneTech Chips
  • Fill the Batcave's vehicle display and wardrobe

Use the Stud Farming Guide if you need to build up Studs for the store — the Stud Cache save-reload method is the fastest route.

Full Checklist

🆕 Story

  • Complete all 6 story chapters
  • Complete all 29+ main missions
  • Unlock all 7 playable characters

🏭 Collectibles

  • Find all 14 in-world Red Bricks
  • Purchase all 9 Bat-Mite Store Red Bricks
  • Find all 10 Batcave Minikits
  • Find all 10 WayneTech Caches
  • Collect all Gold Bricks from missions and open world

🌄 Open World

  • Catch all escaped zoo animals in every district
  • Complete all AR Trials
  • Solve all Riddler Puzzle Rooms
  • Defeat all open-world Villain Encounters
  • Explore 100% of Gotham City's map

📸 Suits & Vehicles

  • Unlock all 101 base-game suits across 7 characters
  • Unlock all vehicles and display them in the Batcave
  • Fill the Batcave wardrobe

☐ Batcave Upgrades

  • Spend all WayneTech Chips on character upgrades
  • Complete all Batcave structural upgrades with Gold Bricks
  • Unlock all Batcave display rooms
Estimated total time: 25–40 hours depending on difficulty chosen, exploration pace, and how efficiently you use Stud Multipliers. The Platinum trophy ("I am Batman") unlocks automatically once all checklist items are complete.
