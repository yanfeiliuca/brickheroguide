Character Playstyle Guide

An overview of the 7 playable characters and their confirmed abilities.

LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight features 7 playable characters, a smaller roster than previous LEGO Batman games. This focused cast means each character has more distinct abilities and a unique playstyle. Here is what we know about each character based on confirmed previews and reviews.

Batman -- The All-Rounder

Batman is the most versatile character, with access to multiple Batsuits that grant different abilities. He can grapple, glide, use Batarangs, and switch between suits to solve puzzles and handle combat situations. Batman is the character you will use for most of the story.

Robin -- The Agile Technician

Robin provides agility-focused gameplay with acrobatic combat moves. He can access areas other characters cannot reach and brings technical abilities for puzzle-solving.

Batgirl -- The Stealth Operative

Batgirl brings stealth-focused abilities to the roster, complementing the more direct combat styles of other heroes. Her skills are useful for missions that reward a quieter approach.

Catwoman -- Whip & Stealth

Catwoman's whip is her signature tool, usable for both combat and traversal. Her stealth capabilities make her valuable in sections where avoiding detection is advantageous.

Commissioner Gordon -- Foam Gun Specialist

Gordon brings a unique foam gun tool that can neutralize certain hazards and enemies. His evidence-gathering abilities help uncover hidden clues and secrets throughout Gotham.

Talia al Ghul -- The Melee Specialist

Talia brings League of Shadows sword combat techniques and parkour abilities to the roster. She is confirmed to be a playable character with a complex narrative role tied to the League of Shadows storyline.

Nightwing -- The Acrobatic Fighter

Nightwing combines fast acrobatic combat with escrima sticks. His agility makes him excellent for navigating complex environments and handling groups of enemies with crowd-control techniques.

Note: Detailed mission-specific character recommendations will be published after the full game launches on May 22, 2026. The story structure and which characters are available in each mission need to be confirmed through full playthroughs.
